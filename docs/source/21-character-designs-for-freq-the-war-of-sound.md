### **FREQ: The War of Sound – Episode 01 Script Sample**

**Title:** **"Opening Set – DJ24 vs. The Sick 52"**

* * *

### **🎵 OPENING SCENE – THE STAGE IS SET**

(*The sound of static fades in, slowly turning into an electrifying bassline.*)

🔊 **NARRATOR (Deep, cinematic voice):***"Welcome to FREQ: The War of Sound. This is more than music. More than a battle. This is a war for control over the frequencies that shape reality itself."*

(*The bass kicks in harder. We hear the sound of a futuristic city—announcements, digital displays, distant subwoofers pulsing in the streets.*)

🔊 **NARRATOR:***"Tonight, we take you to the heart of Ongaku Prime, where the elite warriors of sound, the DJ24, face their most dangerous enemies yet… the exiled mutants of the Sick 52."*

(*The beat pauses. A sharp synth melody kicks in, signaling the start of the battle.*)

* * *

### **🎤 SCENE 1 – THE CONFRONTATION AT ECHO ARENA**

(*A low hum fills the air. We hear boots stepping onto a metallic platform. The crowd murmurs, waiting for the fight to begin.*)

💠 **GENERAL 24 (Commanding, calm):***"This is General 24. Sync, you ready for your first real set?"*

🎧 **SYNC (Cocky, but focused):***"Born ready, General. But uh… is it normal for the bass to feel like it’s growling at me?"*

(*A deep sub-bass rumbles. The air around them distorts slightly. Then… a slow, distorted laugh echoes through the arena.*)

🔴 **RED SILENCE (Cold, eerie whisper):***"You shouldn’t have come here, little DJ. You can’t fight what you can’t hear."*

(*A wave of silence washes over the arena. The sound completely cuts out for a few seconds—total void. Then, a heavy beat drop CRASHES in, shaking the environment.*)

* * *

### **🎵 SCENE 2 – THE SOUND BATTLE BEGINS**

💠 **GENERAL 24:***"Sync, stay on rhythm! He’s trying to erase the beat. Rez, counter with an echo wave!"*

(*We hear a* ***high-frequency synth buildup****, Rez manipulating echoes to push the silence back.*)

🎧 **SYNC (Grinning):***"Oh, you wanna mute me? Let's see how you handle a distortion drop!"*

(*The music switches into* ***glitch-heavy electro, sharp and unpredictable****. Sync manipulates a heavy bass loop, forcing Red Silence’s void to crack open slightly.*)

🔴 **RED SILENCE (Struggling):***"Clever. But not clever enough."*

(*A whisper-thin frequency cuts through Sync’s bassline, splitting the soundwave in half. The Sick 52 fighter* ***Jinx*** *suddenly jumps in.*)

🃏 **JINX (Chaotic, laughing):***"Let’s see how well you battle… BACKWARDS!"*

(*The music starts* ***playing in reverse****, disorienting Sync. His drops land out of sync, his rhythm shatters.*)

🎧 **SYNC (Frustrated, off-balance):***"What the—? My loops! I can’t drop on beat!"*

💠 **GENERAL 24 (Sharply):***"SYNC. ADJUST YOUR TEMPO. USE THE OFFBEAT."*

(*The music shifts again. Sync breathes, refocuses… then starts manually* ***remixing in real-time, countering the reversed sound by warping his tempo.***)

🎧 **SYNC (Grinning, focused):***"Oh, you wanna mess with the beat? Watch me flip this whole set!"*

(*The music suddenly warps into a* ***wild, unpredictable tempo switch****, cutting through Jinx’s distortion. A* ***massive bassline drop SLAMS in.***)

🃏 **JINX (Panicked, glitching):***"Wait—WAIT—NO NO NO—"*

(*Jinx’s frequency shatters. His sound collapses into static as he’s ejected from the battle.*)

🔴 **RED SILENCE (Annoyed, deadly serious):***"Enough. I’ll finish this myself."*

(*The arena goes dark. A single tone echoes—a deadly* ***soundwave pulse, rising into a final, dangerous crescendo.***)

💠 **GENERAL 24:***"Sync! On my count—drop everything you’ve got!"*

🎧 **SYNC:***"Got it—TIME FOR THE FINAL DROP!"*

(*Everything builds… the crowd roars… the music explodes into the final phase of the battle—*)

* * *
