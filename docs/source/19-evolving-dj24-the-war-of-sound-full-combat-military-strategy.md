# **19\. Evolving DJ24: The War of Sound – Full Combat & Military Strategy**

### **Evolving DJ24: The War of Sound – Full Combat & Military Strategy**

Now that you want the **DJ24 and Sick 52 to actually fight with guns, aircraft, and military operations**, we need to **refine their structure, technology, and combat approach**.

✅ **DJ24 is now a full-scale private military force** with bases, helicopters, and aerial deployment.✅ **Sick 52 still fights with music-based combat but also uses guerrilla tactics, ambushes, and tech disruption.**✅ **Both sides have layers of complexity**—DJ24 members **aren’t just soldiers**, they have **personal lives, go to bars, question their orders.**✅ **Should the Sick 52 be set up first or just appear?** **(Answer below!)**

## **1\. The DJ24 Military Structure & Combat Tactics**

DJ24 is no longer just **an elite sound combat unit**—they are now **a highly specialized, futuristic PMC (Private Military Company)** funded by **Billionaire X and his global network.**

### **🔹 How the DJ24 Operates (Tactical Overview)**

-   **Mission-Based Deployment** → They are **sent to global & interdimensional conflict zones** where sound anomalies threaten Ongaku’s control.

-   **Base of Operations** → **The ONYX Tower**, a massive floating airship that serves as **DJ24’s HQ & Command Center.**

-   **Airborne Assault Teams** → Helicopters, **stealth jets, and drop-pod insertions** allow them to reach battle zones fast.

-   **Squad-Based Warfare** → **Each DJ24 member leads a specialized squad**, much like **Special Forces teams (Delta, SEALs, etc.).**

### **🔹 The DJ24 Guard Duty System**

-   When **Ongaku’s major cities need protection**, DJ24 assigns **Guard Duty Squads** to ensure **no rogue Sick 52 activity, NexaGen interference, or Komedian invasions.**

-   Guard Duty is **both a punishment and a test**—low-ranked DJ24 members are assigned **boring city patrols**, while elite warriors handle **VIP protection, corporate security, and black ops.**

-   **Rogue DJ24 members can be assigned to “Ghost Guard” missions**—operations so classified that they **don’t officially exist.**

🔥 **Think of DJ24 as a fusion of:**

-   **The Ghosts from Call of Duty** (Elite, high-tech soldiers)

-   **The UNSC ODST from Halo** (Rapid-response drop-shock units)

-   **The Imperial Guard from Star Wars** (Elite defenders of the ruling order)

## **2\. The Sick 52 – How Do They Enter the Story?**

You asked: **Should they be set up first or just appear suddenly?**

### **Option 1: Setup Before the Action**

📖 **(Slow Burn – Adds More Suspense & Mystery)**

-   We first **see DJ24 operating like a military force, handling routine missions.**

-   The Sick 52 is only **whispered about—rumors, classified reports, unexplained disappearances.**

-   Then, **BOOM! Their first major attack happens**, shocking the world.

### **Option 2: Just Enter the Action**

🔥 **(Fast-Paced – Hits Harder & Feels Like a Warzone)**

-   **Issue #1 STARTS MID-WAR.**

-   The Sick 52 **is already attacking Ongaku Prime**, and **DJ24 is in full military response mode.**

-   No time for build-up—the reader is **thrown straight into the war, learning things as they go.**

**Which option do you prefer?**If you want **fast action**, we go with **Option 2** and throw DJ24 into **a surprise Sick 52 assault.**

## **3\. Expanding DJ24’s Combat – Guns, Aircraft, & Tactics**

Since **DJ24 now fights like a futuristic spec-ops unit**, let’s **give them real weapons & vehicles.**

### **🔹 DJ24 Weaponry & Equipment**

✅ **Sonic Assault Rifles** – Guns that fire **compressed soundwaves** instead of bullets.✅ **Frequency Disruptors** – EMP-style weapons that **scramble soundwaves & AI tech.**✅ **Tactical Drones** – Small hover-drones that **scan, map, and attack enemies from above.**✅ **Blade Bass Knives** – Close-combat knives **that vibrate at extreme frequencies**, cutting through armor.✅ **Portable Subwoofers** – Backpack-sized **bass generators** that **shake the battlefield, stunning enemies.**

### **🔹 DJ24 Aircraft & Vehicles**

✅ **ONYX-Class Dropships** – **Silent, stealth-capable helicopters** for urban operations.✅ **Wave-Striker Jets** – High-speed aircraft using **sonic booms as weapons.**✅ **Ground Assault Buggies** – Fast-moving **hover vehicles** that blast sound cannons.

🔥 **How does DJ24 fight?**

-   **Fast, coordinated strikes**, **airdrop insertions**, and **precision sound warfare.**

-   Their **helicopters & jets use frequency-based weaponry**—they don’t need missiles, just **raw bass energy.**

## **4\. The Sick 52 – Their Combat Style & Weaponry**

Since the **Sick 52 fights in a more chaotic, guerrilla warfare style**, they use:

### **🔹 Sick 52 Tactics & Combat**

✅ **Hit & Run Attacks** – They **never fight fair**, using sound to **disrupt tech & ambush enemies.**✅ **Urban Warfare** – **Booby-trapped sound zones**, hacked speakers, **citywide frequency blackouts.**✅ **Mind Warping Frequencies** – Some Sick 52 members **can drive enemies insane** by **hijacking their neural rhythms.**

### **🔹 Sick 52 Weaponry & Gear**

✅ **Scrap-Tech Sound Blasters** – Their guns are **modded music equipment**, like **guitars that fire plasma waves.**✅ **Hacked NexaGen Armor** – Many wear **stolen exo-suits**, but **badly modified**, making them unstable.✅ **Phantom Bass Mines** – **Explosives made from corrupted sub-bass**, detonating when sound levels drop too low.

🔥 **How does the Sick 52 fight?**

-   Unlike DJ24’s **elite, structured warfare**, they are **unpredictable, savage, and reckless**.

-   They **hack into sound systems, take over cities**, and **use street-level chaos to win battles.**
