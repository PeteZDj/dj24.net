# **5\. The True Nature of DJ24 – Ongaku’s Private Super Army**

### **The True Nature of DJ24 – Ongaku’s Private Super Army**

The **DJ24** aren’t rebels. They are an **elite, world-class force of musically gifted individuals**, trained to **protect Ongaku and its sonic dominion**. Unlike traditional law enforcement or military units, they answer to **no government, no corporation, no council—only themselves**.

Their founder? A **mysterious billionaire known only as "X"**, father of the legendary **General 24**, who established the **Ichiban Private Army**, recruiting **only the most exceptional individuals** in the world.

* * *

## **1\. The Origin of DJ24 – Ichiban’s Vision**

-   **Billionaire X** saw the coming wars—the rise of NexaGen, the corruption of the Harmony Council, the threat of interdimensional sound breaches.

-   He believed that **only those with absolute mastery over sound could keep the world in balance.**

-   Using **his vast wealth**, he created **the Ichiban Program**, an underground recruitment system to find the **greatest musicians, sound warriors, and harmonic prodigies**.

* * *

## **2\. How the DJ24 Operate**

Unlike a traditional army, the **DJ24 operate as a decentralized elite unit**:

-   **24 members** are always active, one for **each hour of the day**, ensuring **constant protection.**

-   **54 reserve members** exist, trained to **rotate in when needed**, maintaining a pool of expertise.

-   Each member is a **one-of-a-kind talent**, specializing in **a unique form of sound combat or musical warfare.**

-   **They operate by their own code**—they do not follow Ongaku’s government, NexaGen, or any outside authority.

* * *

## **3\. Their Role in the XD Era**

The **First XD War** against the **Komedians and Sick 52** forced the DJ24 to **evolve beyond mere planetary defense**.

-   **For the first time, their power wasn’t just needed on Ongaku—it was needed across the galaxy.**

-   The emergence of **Extra-Dimensional Frequencies (XDFs)** meant that threats **could come from anywhere, any reality, any timeline.**

-   **Sync**, the newest recruit, is the **first DJ24 member capable of manipulating XDFs naturally.**

* * *

## **4\. General 24 – The Leader of the DJ24**

**"Music is the language of war. I am its translator."**

-   General 24 is **the son of Billionaire X** and the current **Commander of the DJ24.**

-   A tactical genius, he is **both feared and respected**, known for **eliminating threats before they even begin.**

-   He believes in **absolute efficiency**—**if you don’t belong in DJ24, you are removed.**

-   **His conflict with Sync is inevitable**—while Sync represents **freeform sound evolution**, General 24 represents **structured, precise order.**

* * *

## **5\. The DJ24’s Ultimate Goal**

-   To **preserve the purity of Ongaku's sound-based power** against all threats—external, internal, and interdimensional.

-   To **keep power within their own ranks**—only the **most gifted, the most elite, and the most disciplined** deserve a place in DJ24.

-   To ensure **Ongaku never falls**, even if it means **preemptive strikes against potential threats.**

The question is: **Do they still serve Billionaire X's vision, or have they become something else?**
