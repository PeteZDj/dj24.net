# **3\. The Science Behind the Eras of Ongaku**

### **The Science Behind the Eras of Ongaku**

To make this **feel grounded in physics and scientific principles**, we need to structure how **each era emerged based on real-world acoustics, quantum physics, and wave mechanics**.

* * *

## **1\. The Pre-Rhythm Ages (Before the Low-Res Era)**

**"The Age of Noise" – Before Music, There Was Only Chaos**

-   Before Ongaku’s civilizations formed, **sound existed but was unstructured—just raw noise, vibrations, and primal energy.**

-   Each species (humans, animals, and machines) communicated in **isolated ways**—biological sounds, machine signals, and primitive rhythms.

-   Then, a **discovery was made**: the presence of a **Universal Low-Frequency Oscillator (LFO)**, a **naturally occurring standing wave within Ongaku’s planetary core.**

-   This LFO acted as a **carrier wave** that synchronized all life forms, enabling them to **resonate with a shared rhythm.**

-   **The first harmony was formed**—and thus, the Low-Res Era was born.

### **The Science Behind It:**

-   **Low-Frequency Oscillators (LFOs)** are used in real-world sound synthesis to **modulate frequencies and create patterns**.

-   Ongaku’s LFO was not artificial—it was **a naturally occurring standing wave within the planet’s magnetosphere**, functioning like a planetary-scale resonator.

-   This planetary LFO allowed for **cross-species synchronization**, where biological and mechanical life **aligned to the same sonic structure.**

-   **Analogy:** Just like Earth has the **Schumann Resonance (the electromagnetic frequency of the planet at ~7.83 Hz)**, Ongaku had a **musical equivalent** that linked all life together.

* * *

## **2\. The Low-Res Era – The Golden Age of Pure Music Combat**

**"The Era of Natural Sound and Vibrational Combat"**

-   With the **discovery of the LFO**, civilization evolved around **harmony and competition through sound.**

-   Music became the **dominant form of battle, communication, and cultural expression**.

-   **Genres became schools of thought**, where different tribes mastered different frequencies and rhythms.

-   No advanced technology—**just natural, raw musical battles using voice, instruments, and the body’s own rhythm.**

### **Scientific Explanation:**

-   The LFO allowed for **"Resonant Synchronization"**, meaning people could **channel their energy through sound** and amplify their own biology.

-   The world functioned like a **massive harmonic structure**, where **conflict was resolved through sound-based duels, and cities were built to resonate at specific frequencies.**

-   **Music wasn’t just entertainment—it was a biological necessity.**

#### **How the Low-Res Era Ends:**

-   A disturbance began appearing in the **planetary LFO**—unexplained fluctuations in resonance.

-   Some suspected **natural causes**, others believed **external interference**.

-   **The real truth:** Ongaku was starting to interact with new, **unknown frequencies**, setting the stage for the HD era.

* * *

## **3\. The HD Era – The Era of Technological Control & Oppression**

**"When Sound Became a Weapon"**

-   One day, the **strange fluctuations in the LFO increased**, and the world started observing **new sound anomalies**—frequencies **outside their natural spectrum**.

-   A **visitor arrived from Planet Scientifica**, secretly observing Ongaku’s resonance.

-   This scientist revealed that the **planet’s natural harmonic field was being altered by an unknown cosmic force.**

-   **To study and control this phenomenon, the first sound-based machines were developed.**

### **Scientific Explanation:**

-   The **disruptions** were caused by **an interdimensional interference pattern**—an interaction between Ongaku’s LFO and an external quantum frequency.

-   With the introduction of **scientific tools**, music was no longer purely organic—it became **measurable, quantifiable, and weaponized.**

-   This led to:

-   **The rise of synthetic sound technology** (sound amplifiers, frequency disruptors, and AI-driven music analyzers).

-   **The birth of the Harmony Council**, which sought to regulate all sound activity.

-   The eventual implementation of the **Single Beat System (SBS)**—a universal frequency standard imposed by the ruling class.

#### **How the HD Era Ends:**

-   As the **SBS tightened control**, new **anomalous frequencies** started appearing—this time, at the **quantum level**.

-   The scientist from Scientifica had hinted that Ongaku’s resonance **wasn’t just planetary—it was dimensional.**

-   The government tried to **suppress this knowledge**, but **an experiment went wrong**, and suddenly Ongaku’s sound began reaching **beyond the limits of known physics.**

-   The **first interdimensional sound breach occurred**—marking the start of the XD Era.

* * *

## **4\. The XD Era – The Interdimensional Sound Revolution**

**"When Music Broke the Barriers of Reality"**

-   **10 lifecycles after the HD Era**, the first **cross-dimensional resonance event occurred**.

-   Soundwaves from Ongaku **began to interact with parallel realities**, leading to **the discovery of Extra Dimensional Frequencies (XDFs).**

-   **Sync**, the protagonist of DJ24, is one of the first humans born **naturally attuned to XDFs**, making them capable of **altering sound-based reality.**

### **Scientific Explanation:**

-   Music had now **evolved into a form of quantum resonance**, where certain **frequencies could manipulate not just sound, but space and time itself.**

-   Ongaku’s **LFO was no longer confined to the planet**—it had breached into other dimensions.

-   This led to the discovery of **"Quantum Harmonics"**, which:

-   Allowed **instantaneous sound-based travel** (portals powered by rhythm).

-   Created **sound constructs that could alter matter**.

-   Introduced **an entirely new energy source**, making Ongaku a **center of galactic power.**

#### **The Core Conflict of the XD Era:**

-   The ruling elites (Harmony Council, NexaGen) **want to control and weaponize this power**.

-   Rebels like Sync and the new DJ24 **seek to free music from corporate and political control.**

-   Unknown **alien civilizations** may also be watching, as Ongaku’s resonance starts affecting distant worlds.
