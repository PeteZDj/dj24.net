# 1. 🌍 Races of Planet Ongaku

# **1\. 🌍 Races of Planet Ongaku**

#

Each race embodies a different **philosophy of music and power**, influencing their **abilities, interactions, and culture**.

### **1\. Harmonids (Humans - The Creators) 🎶**

-   **Description:** The **most versatile and adaptive** race, **Harmonids** are the **natural-born musicians** of Ongaku. They **create**, **perform**, and **evolve** through their musical heritage.

-   **Physical Traits:** Standard humanoid, varying in **appearance, skill, and specialization**.

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Genre Mastery** – Can **adapt to different music styles** faster than other races.

-   **Endless Inspiration** – Gains **bonuses for creativity**, making them **highly adaptable** in combat and social interactions.

-   **Culture:** They **dominate the music industry** on Ongaku, acting as **composers, performers, and leaders** in the arts.

-   **Best Classes:** **Any class works** due to their adaptability.

* * *

### **2\. Mechavibes (AI/Machine - The Programmers) 🤖**

-   **Description:** These are **artificial beings**—robots, androids, or AI-driven sentients—who interpret **music mathematically**, wielding **sound as data, energy, and force**.

-   **Physical Traits:** Mechanical bodies **powered by soundwaves**, often integrated with **digital instruments** or **resonance-based armor**.

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Waveform Manipulation** – Converts **sound into pure energy**, fueling attacks or defenses.

-   **Auto-Tune Regeneration** – Can **self-repair over time**, making them **resilient in prolonged battles**.

-   **Culture:** Once created as **tools for performance enhancement**, they **gained sentience**, now forming their own **tech-based underground collectives**.

-   **Best Classes:** **Mage** (using digital sound magic) or **Warrior** (as a tanky cyborg).

* * *

### **3\. Sonari (Animal-Hybrids - The Instinctual) 🦅🎵**

-   **Description:** These are **anthropomorphic animals**—beasts that evolved **alongside music**, using their **natural sound-producing abilities** to fight and perform.

-   **Physical Traits:** **Birds, reptiles, felines, canines, and more**, with **natural instruments built into their bodies** (e.g., birds with flute-like vocal cords, lizards with deep bass frequencies).

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Natural Resonance** – Innate **sound-based powers** unique to their **species** (e.g., birds = high-frequency attacks, felines = rhythmic agility).

-   **Sonic Reflexes** – React faster **when moving in sync with the beat** of the environment.

-   **Culture:** They live **in the wild outskirts of Ongaku**, thriving in **freeform music cultures** like **jazz, punk, and folk**.

-   **Best Classes:** **Warrior** (instinct-driven fighters) or **Mage** (channeling primal sound magic).

* * *

# **⚔️ Classes of Planet Ongaku**

Each class wields music in a **unique way**, affecting their **combat, movement, and interaction styles**.

### **1\. Bass Bruiser (Warrior) 🥁**

-   **Description:** Masters of **percussive force**, Bass Bruisers **use the power of rhythm and beats** to enhance their strength, endurance, and combat abilities.

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Beatdown** – Uses **percussive strikes** to create **shockwaves** and **disrupt enemies**.

-   **Drop the Bass** – Can **slam the ground**, sending **wave-like tremors** that knock back foes.

-   **Resonant Armor** – Gains **damage resistance** by syncing with the battle’s rhythm.

-   **Best Races:** **Harmonids & Sonari** (instinct-driven combat works well with rhythm-based attacks).

* * *

### **2\. Synthcaster (Mage) 🎹**

-   **Description:** The **masters of electronic magic**, Synthcasters wield **digital frequencies, harmonic spells, and waveform distortion** as weapons.

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Glitch Phase** – Can **phase-shift through sound waves**, allowing temporary **intangibility**.

-   **Melody Hack** – **Disrupts enemy abilities**, making them temporarily **off-beat and confused**.

-   **Reverb Shield** – Creates **a sound barrier** that deflects attacks or **echoes magic spells** back at enemies.

-   **Best Races:** **Mechavibes & Harmonids** (machine precision and human creativity make for powerful spellcasters).

* * *

### **3\. Tempo Striker (Hybrid - Rogue/Speed Fighter) 🎤**

-   **Description:** Agile **speed-based fighters** who **weave between beats**, using rapid **combos, dashes, and deceptive movements**.

-   **Abilities:**

-   **Speed Riff** – Can **chain multiple attacks** in succession, **increasing damage the longer they stay in rhythm**.

-   **Sonic Step** – Moves **faster with music**, allowing near-instant **evasive maneuvers**.

-   **Rewind Echo** – Can **reverse a few seconds of time**, dodging an attack or repeating a previous move.

-   **Best Races:** **Sonari & Harmonids** (natural agility and rhythmic mastery make them perfect for this playstyle).
