# **9\. The First XD War – The Komedians & The Sick 52 Attack Ongaku**

### **The First XD War – The Komedians & The Sick 52 Attack Ongaku**

**"When the multiverse opened, the first invaders weren’t warriors. They were comedians."**

* * *

## **1\. How the War Started – The Komedians Discover Extra-Dimensional Frequencies (XDFs)**

During the **early XD era**, Ongaku's scientists and rebels were still experimenting with **Extra-Dimensional Frequencies (XDFs)**—soundwaves that interacted with **parallel realities**.

-   While testing an **XDF resonance beacon**, Ongaku accidentally **broadcasted a planetary-wide musical signal into another dimension**.

-   This signal **pierced through the dimensional veil** and reached **Planet Joke**—a world where comedy and satire ruled.

-   The inhabitants of **Planet Joke**, known as **The Komedians**, received the signal and **mistook it for an invitation**—or worse, an insult.

* * *

## **2\. Who Are the Komedians?**

-   The **Komedians** are an entire civilization built around **jokes, pranks, and satire**, but **not all comedy is harmless**.

-   They believe **humor is the most powerful weapon in the universe**, capable of **disrupting minds and reshaping reality itself.**

-   Their ruling government, known as **The Grand Punchline**, saw Ongaku's XDF signal as **"the most unfunny sound ever heard"**—and took it as a **declaration of war**.

-   They decided that **Ongaku must be "fixed"**—either by turning the planet into a **comedy empire** or **destroying it completely.**

* * *

## **3\. The Komedians Make Contact with the Sick 52**

While preparing their **assault on Ongaku**, the Komedians **searched for local allies** who also hated the planet’s leadership.

-   They found the **Sick 52**, a group of **exiled musicians and sonic mutants** who were still recovering from their defeat during the **DJ24 rebellions of the HD era.**

-   The **Komedians offered the Sick 52 a deal**:

-   In exchange for **helping them overthrow Ongaku**, the Komedians would grant the Sick 52 access to **reality-warping humor technology**—a power that could **corrupt and distort sound beyond repair**.

-   The Sick 52 **saw this as their ultimate chance for revenge**.

Together, they formed the **XD Chaos Front**, a coalition dedicated to **destroying Ongaku’s influence over sound-based reality.**

* * *

## **4\. The XD War Begins – The Attack on Ongaku**

The Komedians, armed with **reality-warping joke tech**, **sound distortion weapons**, and **Sick 52 mutants**, launched a **full-scale assault on Ongaku.**

### **Key Events of the XD War:**

-   **The Bassline Collapse** – The Komedians **bombed Ongaku’s Resonance Towers**, causing a **planet-wide desynchronization** in music. For the first time, **Ongaku lost its universal rhythm**.

-   **Harmonic Insanity Virus** – Using **laughter-based sonic warfare**, the Komedians unleashed a **frequency that made musicians play uncontrollably**, sending entire cities into **chaos and absurdity**.

-   **The Silence Requiem** – The Sick 52, with new **Komedian tech**, developed a **reverse soundwave that erased entire songs and memories**. They used it to **wipe out entire genres from existence**.

* * *

## **5\. The DJ24 Respond – Sync and the New Revolution**

### **The Battle for the Future of Music**

As Ongaku **descended into chaos**, the DJ24, led by **Sync**, rallied the planet’s last defenders.

-   **Sync discovered that the Komedians weren’t just attacking for fun—they were rewriting reality itself.**

-   If left unchecked, **music itself could be rewritten into an endless joke**—or erased entirely.

-   Using **advanced XDF sound weapons**, the DJ24 fought to **restore balance**, battling both **Komedian warlords and Sick 52 rebels** in a final showdown.

* * *

## **6\. How the XD War Ends**

### **The Ultimate Sonic Battle – Frequency vs. Absurdity**

The final battle saw **Sync and the DJ24 infiltrating the Komedians' main ship**, The Laugh Track, where they faced off against **The Grand Punchline** and the most powerful Sick 52 mutants.

-   Sync **used their unique XDF power** to counteract the **harmonic distortions**, stabilizing Ongaku’s planetary frequency.

-   The Sick 52 leader, **Red Silence**, attempted to unleash the **Final Joke**, a frequency that would have turned **all of Ongaku into an endless loop of nonsense sound.**

-   In a final **DJ battle that altered reality itself**, Sync defeated the **Grand Punchline and Red Silence**, forcing the Komedians into retreat.

* * *

## **7\. The Aftermath – A Universe Forever Changed**

-   The Komedians **retreated into their own dimension**, but **left behind fragments of their joke technology**, meaning **Ongaku is forever altered**.

-   The Sick 52 **were scattered, but some survivors still seek revenge.**

-   **The DJ24 proved that music was still the dominant force**, but the war showed that Ongaku is **no longer just a planet—it is now a beacon for interdimensional sound battles.**

-   The universe **realized that XDF soundwaves can alter reality itself**, meaning Ongaku is now a **target for even greater cosmic threats.**
