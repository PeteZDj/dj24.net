# **20\. Creating the Webcomic Preview – The First 5-10 Pages**

20.Creating the Webcomic Preview – The First 5-10 Pages

### **Creating the Webcomic Preview – The First 5-10 Pages**

Since we’re starting with a **Webcomic Preview**, we need:✅ **A cinematic opening scene** – Something that instantly hooks readers.✅ **A short but powerful first battle** – Showcase the world’s unique fighting style.✅ **A cliffhanger ending** – Make people want the full series.✅ **A format optimized for Webtoon/Tapas** – **Vertical scrolling panels** for easy mobile reading.

* * *

## **1\. The Plot of the Preview Chapter – "The First Drop"**

This **introduces the world of Ongaku**, the rivalry between **DJ24 and the Sick 52**, and sets up the **war over sound control.**

### **📖 Summary of Chapter 1 Preview:**

-   📍 **Setting:** Ongaku Prime – *The Echo Arena, an underground battleground where sound duels are fought.*

-   🎙️ **Main Characters:**

-   **SYNC (DJ24 Rookie)** – New recruit, unsure of his true abilities.

-   **RED SILENCE (Sick 52 Leader)** – The most feared frequency manipulator, erasing sound itself.

-   **GENERAL 24 (DJ24 Leader)** – Watching the battle from a command center, judging Sync’s potential.

-   🎵 **Conflict:** Sync must prove himself in **his first real battle**, but **Red Silence is more powerful than he expected.**

-   🔥 **Cliffhanger Ending:** Sync barely survives, but Red Silence **whispers a cryptic message before disappearing:**

    *"You're not their savior, kid. You're their weapon."*

* * *

## **2\. Storyboard Breakdown – The First 5-10 Pages**

Each **page is formatted for fast, immersive scrolling**, building momentum toward the first **massive drop.**

### **🔹 Page 1 – Establishing the World**

-   **Panel 1:** *A dark cyberpunk cityscape, neon lights reflecting off rain-covered streets.*

-   **Panel 2:** *A low-frequency hum fills the air. A bass drop echoes from a distant building.*

-   **Panel 3:** *Cut to a massive underground arena—THE ECHO ARENA.*

-   **Panel 4:** *A digital display lights up:*

    🔊 **"LIVE BATTLE: DJ24 vs. The Sick 52"**

* * *

### **🔹 Page 2-3 – The Fighters Enter the Arena**

-   **Panel 1:** *SYNC steps onto the stage, wearing his DJ24 headset and battle gauntlets.*

-   **Panel 2:** *A voice booms over the speakers:*

    **"New recruit versus the deadliest sound manipulator alive. Let’s see if you can stay on beat, rookie."**

-   **Panel 3:** *Opposite Sync, RED SILENCE walks in—calm, confident, no visible speakers or gear.*

-   **Panel 4:** *Sync grips his gauntlets, nervous.*

-   **SYNC (inner monologue):** *No decks, no speakers? How does he fight?*

-   **Panel 5:** *Red Silence raises his hand. His fingers flicker slightly, and the entire arena goes silent.*

* * *

### **🔹 Page 4-6 – The Battle Begins (Sound Erasure Attack)**

-   **Panel 1:** *SYNC hesitates as the audience noise fades away—total silence.*

-   **Panel 2:** *Red Silence closes his eyes. The screen in the arena glitches. Words disappear. The entire environment loses all sound.*

-   **Panel 3:** *Sync panics, trying to drop a beat—but nothing plays.*

-   **SYNC:** *"What the hell—my speakers aren’t working!?"*

-   **Panel 4:** *Red Silence smirks, steps forward.*

-   **RED SILENCE (whispering):** *"You’re already losing, rookie."*

-   **Panel 5:** *Suddenly, Red Silence SLAMS his foot down—sending a pure frequency WAVE across the arena. The impact CRASHES into Sync, throwing him backward.*

* * *

### **🔹 Page 7-9 – Sync Fights Back (Bass Counterattack)**

-   **Panel 1:** *Sync hits the ground, gasping, his vision flickering.*

-   **SYNC (inner monologue):** *I can’t hear. I can’t fight. I have to—FEEL IT.*

-   **Panel 2:** *He closes his eyes, touches the ground, syncing with the vibrations.*

-   **Panel 3:** *The camera zooms into his heartbeat—Sync starts tapping his fingers.*

-   **SFX: Bum… bum… bum-bum-bum.**

-   **Panel 4:** *Suddenly, the ground beneath him THUMPS—his rhythm begins resonating with the arena itself.*

-   **Panel 5:** *Red Silence pauses, sensing the shift.*

-   **Panel 6:** *SYNC OPENS HIS EYES—his body PULSING WITH A LOW-FREQUENCY BASSLINE.*

-   **SYNC:** *"You wanna erase sound? Then let’s make some noise."*

-   **Panel 7:** *He SLAMS his foot down—BASS DROP. The arena SHAKES as Sync launches a SUBWAVE ATTACK, sending a massive shockwave toward Red Silence.*

* * *

### **🔹 Page 10 – The Cliffhanger Ending**

-   **Panel 1:** *Red Silence takes the hit—his body flickers like an afterimage.*

-   **Panel 2:** *For the first time, he looks surprised.*

-   **Panel 3:** *The sound slowly returns to the arena. Sync is still standing.*

-   **Panel 4:** *Red Silence smirks, stepping back into the shadows.*

-   **RED SILENCE:** *"You might actually be interesting."*

-   **Panel 5:** *As he fades away, Sync hears his final words:*

-   **RED SILENCE (whispering):** *"You're not their savior, kid. You're their weapon."*

-   **Panel 6:** *Sync breathes heavily, realizing he has no idea what Red Silence meant. General 24 watches from the shadows.*

-   **Panel 7:** *Final text box: "To Be Continued…"*

## **4\. How to Get an Artist for the Webcomic**

To get this drawn, we can:

1️⃣ **Hire an artist from ArtStation, DeviantArt, or Fiverr**

-   Prices range from **$30–$300 per page**, depending on style.

-   For a **5-10 page preview, budget around $200–$1000.**

2️⃣ **Use AI for Concept Art, Then Have an Artist Refine It**

-   AI tools like MidJourney or Stable Diffusion can **generate rough layouts.**

-   An artist can then **polish them into final manga panels.**

3️⃣ **Collaborate with an Up-and-Coming Manga Artist**

-   Find someone who loves **Cyberpunk, DJ culture, and shonen anime.**

-   Offer a **revenue split or Patreon profit share** to make it a long-term project.
