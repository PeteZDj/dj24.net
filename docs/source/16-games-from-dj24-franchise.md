# **16\. Games from Dj24 Franchise**

## **1\. DJ Battle (Low-Res Era) – Classic Sound Combat, Pre-HD Age**

🔹 **Inspirations:** **Pokémon | Age of Empires | Naruto/Mortal Kombat | ???**🔹 **Genre Mix:** **Turn-based or tactical combat | City-building mechanics | Tournament-style progression | Rhythm-based duels**

### **Core Gameplay Features**

-   **Tactical Sound Combat:** Like Pokémon but with **music duels instead of creature battles**.

-   **Training & Mastery:** Each faction/genre has unique **combat styles and schools**, similar to Naruto’s jutsu or Mortal Kombat’s martial arts.

-   **City-Building & Influence:** Players develop **a small city or guild**, similar to Age of Empires, unlocking **stronger sound warriors**.

-   **Rhythm-Based Fights:** Battles are **timing-based**, requiring **quick inputs and strategic sound control**.

-   **Exploration & Faction Rivalries:** Instead of a free-roam open world, the game may use **an overworld map with different regions**, each ruled by a **music genre-based dojo or master**.

#### **Possible Missing Element:**

-   Something like **Tekken or Street Fighter** could work here if you want **direct control over combat mechanics** in fights.

* * *

## **2\. DJ24 (XD Era) – Open-World, Cyberpunk, Space Age War**

🔹 **Inspirations:** **Cyberpunk 2077 (+Edgerunners) | EVE Online/No Man’s Sky/Star Citizen | GTA | Age of Empires | DJ24’s Internal Politics**🔹 **Genre Mix:** **Open-world RPG | Space exploration | City-Building | Tactical & Rhythm Combat | Political Intrigue**

### **Core Gameplay Features**

-   **Open-World & Free Movement:** More like **Cyberpunk/GTA** but within **expanding cities and interstellar locations**.

-   **City-Building at Scale:** Unlike DJ Battle’s small dojo cities, DJ24 focuses on **controlling and managing entire planets** through diplomacy or war.

-   **Faction Warfare & Political Intrigue:** Think **EVE Online**, where different power groups (NexaGen, DJ24, Komedians, etc.) **fight for dominance**.

-   **Tactical Combat with Real-Time Action:** Cyberpunk + Edgerunners influences allow **fast-paced fights**, but musical attacks still have **strategy & timing elements**.

-   **Multiplayer & Galaxy Expansion:** Inspired by **No Man’s Sky/EVE Online**, where players can **travel between planets** and control **their own fleets, crews, or armies**.

#### **Possible Missing Element:**

-   A **Total War-style planetary battle system** could be included, where DJ24 engages in large-scale warfare for **planetary control**.
