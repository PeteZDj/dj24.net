## **2\. The Format – How It’s Released Online**

### **✅ Webtoon / Webcomic Structure**

Since you want this as a **weekly/bi-weekly webcomic**, we should follow a **Webtoon/Manhwa format:**

-   **Each episode = 50-80 panels** (to keep engagement high).

-   **Vertical scrolling format** (for seamless mobile reading).

-   **Action-heavy sequences broken into multiple episodes** (so fights have build-up).

### **✅ Art Style**

We need an art style that fits the **Cyberpunk + Shonen Anime feel.**

**Style**

**Influences**

**Manga Black & White**

Naruto, Jujutsu Kaisen, Chainsaw Man

**Digital Cyberpunk Color**

Cyberpunk 2077, Bladerunner, Edgerunners

**Stylized Glitch Art**

Experimental lighting/effects like Akira

For **fast releases**, a mix of **black & white with colored highlights** (like Tron/Edgerunners) could work.
