# 2. The History of Planet Ongaku (A Dual-Timeline Approach)

# **2\. The History of Planet Ongaku (A Dual-Timeline Approach)**

## **The History of Planet Ongaku (A Dual-Timeline Approach)**

### **Era 1: The Age of Harmony (Low-Res Game – The Past)**

**"Before the wars, before the cities, music was pure."**

-   Planet Ongaku was once a **spiritually connected world**, where music was **not just entertainment but a force of nature**.

-   Every region had its **own style of music**, and people **battled with rhythm and melodies**, not weapons.

-   This was the **Golden Age of Sound Combat**—fights weren’t about destruction but **dueling through music** to prove artistic mastery.

-   However, some **believed in restricting certain genres** to create a **"Perfect Melody"**, leading to **conflict among musicians**.

#### **Game Focus:**

-   No guns or advanced tech, only **sound-based combat** (turntables, instruments, vocal battles).

-   The **rivalries** were about **honor and artistic mastery**, not war.

-   This era ended with **The Silence War**, when the ruling factions **tried to control music completely**—which leads into the next era.

* * *

### **Era 2: The Silence War (Transition Period)**

**"When they controlled sound, they controlled the people."**

-   A major **worldwide conflict** erupted as the ruling powers attempted to **erase musical diversity** and create a **single, controlled beat for all of Ongaku**.

-   **New technology was invented** to weaponize music, turning **instruments into tools of war**.

-   Some **musicians became rebels**, fighting to restore freedom.

-   **This is the point where shooting mechanics start emerging**—music-based guns, sound cannons, and frequency disruptors.

* * *

### **Era 3: The Age of Control (High-Res Game – The Future, DJ24 Era)**

**"The winners rewrote history. The losers were forgotten. But music never truly dies."**

-   The **Harmony Council** emerged victorious, creating the **Single Beat System (SBS)** that dictates **what music is allowed** and **what is forbidden**.

-   **Ongaku Prime became a megacity**, where the elite control music distribution, and underground movements fight to restore lost genres.

-   New tech allows for **firearms, AI musicians, cybernetic enhancements**, and **weaponized sound battles**.

-   **The DJ24 exists in this era**, using both **classic sound combat** and **high-tech gear** to fight back.

#### **Game Focus:**

-   Open-world city-building mechanics.

-   **Guns, hacking, cyber-DJs, tech-based abilities.**

-   Fighting against **the corrupt system** that controls music.

-   A more chaotic and fast-paced battle system.

* * *

## **How This Works for You**

-   **Low-Res Game = The Past** → A prequel set in the **Golden Age of Music Combat**, before guns and cybernetic warfare existed.

-   **High-Res DJ24 Game = The Future** → A full cyberpunk rebellion where tech and weapons have changed the nature of music battles.

This way, the **low-res game can be more traditional** (music duels, rhythm combat, classic RPG mechanics), while the **high-res game is full-on futuristic** (guns, AI battles, cybernetic warfare).
