# 6. The Internal Politics of DJ24 – Power Struggles Within the Ultimate Sound Army

# **6\. The Internal Politics of DJ24 – Power Struggles Within the Ultimate Sound Army**

While the **DJ24** are **the elite private army of Ongaku**, they are **not unified in ideology**. The **Ichiban Program** ensured that only the **greatest, most gifted individuals** could join—but being powerful doesn’t mean they all agree.

Over time, **internal conflicts have emerged**, leading to **rivalries, factions, and even betrayals**.

* * *

## **1\. The Three Factions Within DJ24**

Though they all **serve under General 24**, there are **three competing philosophies** on how the DJ24 should operate.

### **🔵 The Harmonic Order – “Structure, Unity, Control.”**

**Leader:** **General 24Belief:** DJ24 must operate **like an elite military force**, following a **structured, hierarchical system** where power is controlled and sound remains disciplined.

-   **They believe in preemptive action**—if someone is a **potential threat**, eliminate them before they can cause damage.

-   They favor **precise, controlled music combat**, using **synchronized formations** and military-level discipline.

-   Many **senior, veteran members** of DJ24 are part of this faction, as they see themselves as the **guardians of Ongaku’s future**.

**Clashes with Other Factions:**

-   **They see The Mavericks as reckless and dangerous.**

-   **They fear The Resonant Sons might betray DJ24 in favor of “free music.”**

* * *

### **🔴 The Mavericks – “We Make the Rules.”**

**Leader:** **Drez, the Unchained Beat** (DJ24’s most rebellious member)**Belief:** The DJ24 shouldn’t just **protect Ongaku**—they should **rule it**.

-   They believe that **Ongaku Prime’s government, NexaGen, and all the old powers are weak**.

-   Instead of **reacting to threats**, they want to **become the ultimate force in sound warfare**, taking control of Ongaku completely.

-   This faction consists of **younger, aggressive members**, many of whom were once **street fighters, rogue DJs, or rebels who were “tamed” into the DJ24.**

-   They **use unpredictable battle tactics**, bending and distorting sound combat rules.

**Clashes with Other Factions:**

-   **They openly defy The Harmonic Order, believing that DJ24 should answer to no one.**

-   **They see The Resonant Sons as “soft” and naive for thinking music should be free.**

* * *

### **🟢 The Resonant Sons – “Music is Freedom, Not a Weapon.”**

**Leader:** **Sync** (if they prove themselves) / A senior DJ24 member who secretly opposes General 24.**Belief:** The **original purpose** of DJ24 wasn’t to control music—it was to **protect it**.

-   They believe **DJ24 has gone too far**—instead of **preserving music**, they have **become another force of oppression**.

-   They want to **restore freedom to music**, even if it means **breaking away from General 24’s rule**.

-   Many **older members of DJ24** secretly support this faction, as they remember **Billionaire X’s original vision** and fear General 24 is losing his way.

-   **They are conflicted about Sync**—some see them as the key to change, others **don’t trust someone born with natural XDF abilities**.

**Clashes with Other Factions:**

-   **They oppose The Harmonic Order’s authoritarian rule.**

-   **They fear The Mavericks will only replace one dictatorship with another.**

* * *

## **2\. Power Struggles – How DJ24’s Internal Politics Affect the World**

-   **General 24 is aware of the division but refuses to acknowledge it openly.**

-   While DJ24 still operates **as a single force**, individual members **secretly align with different factions**.

-   This means that **missions don’t always go as planned**—some members may **sabotage operations, help rebels, or even leak information to the enemy.**

-   In the upcoming **XD conflicts**, DJ24 **isn’t just fighting external enemies**—they are **slowly fracturing from within.**

* * *

## **3\. Major Conflicts That Could Occur**

### **🚨 A DJ24 Civil War?**

-   If tensions reach a breaking point, **DJ24 might split**, leading to a **battle between the factions**.

-   The **Harmony Council and NexaGen would exploit this weakness**, possibly supporting one faction over the others.

-   **Sync’s actions will determine if DJ24 collapses or finds a new path.**

### **🤯 Secret Assassinations & Betrayals**

-   If General 24 believes certain members are **traitors**, they might **silently remove them** before the conflict escalates.

-   **The Mavericks could stage a coup**, attempting to **take over DJ24 from the inside**.

-   The **Resonant Sons may try to expose DJ24’s darkest secrets**, causing a crisis of faith within the organization.

### **🛑 What Happens If DJ24 Falls?**

-   Without DJ24, **Ongaku would be vulnerable to NexaGen, the Komedians, or other external threats.**

-   **If the Mavericks take control, Ongaku could turn into a dictatorship.**

-   **If the Resonant Sons win, they might try to disband DJ24 entirely.**

-   **If The Harmonic Order remains in power, DJ24 may become even more authoritarian.**

* * *

This internal conflict makes DJ24 **more than just an elite team**—they are a **ticking time bomb of power, ideology, and control**.

1.  **Will Sync unite them, or will they destroy themselves?**

2.  **Who really controls DJ24—General 24, or the hidden influence of Billionaire X?**

3.  **Is DJ24 still Ongaku’s greatest protector, or has it become the very thing it was meant to fight?**
