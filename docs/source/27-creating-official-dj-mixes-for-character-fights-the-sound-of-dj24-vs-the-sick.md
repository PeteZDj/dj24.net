# **27\. Creating Official DJ Mixes for Character Fights – The Sound of DJ24 vs. The Sick 52**

### **Creating Official DJ Mixes for Character Fights – The Sound of DJ24 vs. The Sick 52**

Now that we have the **world, characters, and fighting styles**, we need to **bring it to life through sound**. This means producing **official DJ mixes** that represent:

✅ **Each faction’s musical identity** (DJ24, Sick 52, NexaGen, etc.)✅ **Individual characters’ battle themes** (Like anime fight OSTs, but DJ remixes)✅ **The pacing of fights through drops, breakdowns, and buildups**✅ **Signature sound techniques represented through mixing & effects**

* * *

## **1\. What Does a Fight DJ Mix Sound Like?**

The **best reference** would be a fusion of:

-   **Anime fight music (Attack on Titan, Naruto, Bleach, Cyberpunk: Edgerunners OSTs)**

-   **Cyberpunk club mixes (Justice, Daft Punk, The Glitch Mob, Gesaffelstein, REZZ, Carpenter Brut)**

-   **Festival-level DJ drops (Skrillex, ZHU, RL Grime, Noisia, Apashe, TOKiMONSTA)**

* * *

## **2\. Mix Breakdown – Structure of a DJ Battle Track**

Every **fight mix** will feel like an **actual battle**, with **phases** that tell the story of the fight through sound.

**Section**

**What’s Happening in the Fight?**

**Sound / Mixing Style**

**Intro (0:00 – 1:00)**

Fighters enter, tension builds

Low bass, cinematic strings, deep ambience

**Phase 1 (1:00 – 2:30)**

First attacks exchanged

Driving beat, pulsing synths, aggressive chops

**Phase 2 (2:30 – 4:00)**

Mid-fight, counterattacks

Complex percussion, glitchy edits, tension rises

**Breakdown (4:00 – 5:00)**

One fighter gains the upper hand

Slowed tempo, atmospheric build-up

**Final Phase (5:00 – 6:30)**

The final attack

Huge drop, distorted bass, climactic melody

**Outro (6:30 – 7:00)**

Victory or cliffhanger

Fading pads, echoing synths

## **3\. First Three Battle Mixes to Create**

To start, we can make three **character vs. character DJ mixes**, each representing a **different fighting style**.

* * *

### **🎵 MIX 1: "SYNC vs. JINX – The Tempo Shift Battle"**

🔥 **Fighting Concept:** Jinx manipulates **reversed sound loops**, forcing Sync to **counter with glitch-speed rhythm shifts**.

**Sound Style:**✅ **Glitch-heavy electro** (think REZZ, The Glitch Mob)✅ **Reversed synths & chopped vocals**✅ **Unexpected BPM shifts (speed-ups and slowdowns)**

**Track Moments:**

-   **(Intro)** Distant distorted laughter, eerie pads, Sync entering.

-   **(Drop 1)** Jinx flips the music **backwards** mid-drop, making it feel disorienting.

-   **(Drop 2)** Sync counters by **doubling the tempo**, bringing the energy back.

-   **(Final Battle Drop)** Sync fully controls the rhythm, **overriding Jinx’s chaos**.

* * *

### **🎵 MIX 2: "BASS PHANTOM vs. NOVA – Sub-Bass Destruction"**

🔥 **Fighting Concept:** Bass Phantom’s **heartbeat is a sub-bass cannon**, while Nova fights with **high-energy synthwave laser attacks**.

**Sound Style:**✅ **Heavy dubstep & bass music** (think Skrillex, Noisia, Subtronics)✅ **Low-end sub movement** (earthquake bass)✅ **Bright, piercing synth leads**

**Track Moments:**

-   **(Intro)** The sound of **a heartbeat** slowly becoming distorted.

-   **(Drop 1)** Bass Phantom slams the arena with **a 30Hz sub hit** that rattles everything.

-   **(Mid-Fight Shift)** Nova introduces **high-frequency arpeggios**, cutting through the bass.

-   **(Final Drop)** A fusion of **low-end and high-frequency energy**, causing a massive bass-melody explosion.

* * *

### **🎵 MIX 3: "GENERAL 24 vs. RED SILENCE – The Final Symphony"**

🔥 **Fighting Concept:** General 24 fights with **military precision and structured rhythm**, while Red Silence **uses void sound to erase music itself**.

**Sound Style:**✅ **Orchestral cyber-electro fusion** (think Two Steps From Hell x Cyberpunk OST)✅ **Heavy kicks with militant snare patterns**✅ **Deep, low void sounds fading in and out**

**Track Moments:**

-   **(Intro)** Haunting choir pads, the sound of **marching footsteps**, Red Silence whispers.

-   **(Drop 1)** General 24 strikes first—**pulsing kicks and precise rhythms** dominate.

-   **(Mid-Fight Shift)** Red Silence cuts out all sound except for **low sub rumbles**.

-   **(Final Drop)** General 24 counterattacks, **bringing the full symphony back with power**.
