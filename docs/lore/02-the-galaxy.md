# 02 · The Galaxy — Single-Craft Planets

> Source: `source/4-the-planets-of-the-galaxy.md`

The galaxy is organized so that **each planet specializes in one craft or industry** and is defined
by its main export. Ongaku (music) is the cultural superpower; the others provide the surrounding
universe and future story/expansion hooks (especially for the *Planet Ongaku* galactic-era game and
Seasons 3–4 travel arcs).

| # | Planet | Craft / Export | Core tension |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Ongaku** 🎶 | Music; sound-tech, musicians, music-powered energy | Government control vs. underground rebels |
| 2 | **Joke** 🎭 | Comedy/satire (home of the Komedians; ~150M comedians) | A faction purging "unfunny" comedians |
| 3 | **TerraScripta** 📖 | Writers & storytellers; books, scripts, records | Rumored "Forbidden Stories" that rewrite reality |
| 4 | **Pixela** 🎨 | Digital artists & game designers; VR, concept art | Traditional vs. AI-art purists; the AI "Blank Canvas" |
| 5 | **Gastronomica** 🍽️ | Chefs & food scientists; spices, cuisine | Natural vs. lab-engineered food; banned-flavor market |
| 6 | **Agrisole** 🌱 | Farmers & biologists; plant life, eco-tech | Mega-corps vs. farmers; rumored sentient plant race |
| 7 | **Zenithar** 🏺 | Crafters & artisans; handmade goods, custom weapons | Mass production vs. tradition |
| 8 | **Scientifica** 🔬 | Scientists & innovators; AI, futuristic weapons | "Purists" vs. "Evolvers"; a vanished self-aware AI |
| 9 | **Ecomerica** 💰 | Traders & tycoons; finance, trade | Corporate overlords vs. a funded rebellion |
| 10 | **Harmonia** 💃 | Dancers & performers; performing arts | Fear of AI-generated performance |
| 11 | **Fabriqua** ⚙️ | Engineers & builders; machinery, robotics, infrastructure | Automation vs. skilled labor |
| 12 | **Cura** 🏥 | Doctors & healers; medicine, biogenetics | Genetic enhancement vs. natural evolution |
| 13 | **Chronos** ⏳ | Historians & timekeepers; archives, time-tech | A faction wanting to erase certain truths |
| 14 | **Infinispace** 🌌 | Explorers & astronomers; star maps, transport | Free exploration vs. licensed-only travel |

## Story connectivity

- **Planet Scientifica** is recurring and important: a Scientifica visitor first detects the LFO
  disturbance that opens the **HD Era**, and **Dr. Aulon** (Scientifica) delivers the Komedian
  warning in **Season 2**.
- **Planet Joke** is the Komedians' homeworld; Sync is taken there in Season 2 (the "Joke Arena").
- **Ecomerica, Fabriqua, Chronos** are visited by General 24 and Billionaire X in **Season 3** as the
  war winds down and the saga opens toward the galactic era.
