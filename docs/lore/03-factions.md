## 🛡️ DJ24 — Ongaku's Private Super Army

**"Structure, Unity, Control."**

- **Not rebels** — an elite, world-class force of musically gifted individuals who protect Ongaku's
  sonic dominion and **answer to no government, corporation, or council — only themselves.**
- **Founded by Billionaire "X"** (father of **General 24**) via the **Ichiban Program**, an
  underground recruitment system for the greatest sound warriors.
- **Structure:** **24 active guardians** (one per hour of the day, always-on protection) + **54
  reserves** who rotate in. Each member is a one-of-a-kind talent.
- **Goal:** preserve the purity of Ongaku's sound-power against all threats — external, internal, and
  interdimensional — keeping power within the most elite ranks, even via preemptive strikes.
- **Central tension:** Are they protectors, or just another oppressive force? Do they still serve
  Billionaire X's vision, or have they become something else?

**Internal factions** (see `06`/`source/6`):
- **The Harmonic Order** — led by General 24; structure, unity, military discipline.
- **The Mavericks** — led by Drez "the Unchained Beat"; chaotic, want to *rule* Ongaku, not just protect it.
- **The Resonant Sons** — led by Sync/veterans; believe music should be free; oppose authoritarian control.

Full roster in `04-dj24-roster.md`.

## 💀 The Sick 52 — Exiled Maestros Turned Mutants

**"They exiled us. They erased us. Now we erase them."**

- Once the **Sonic Order** — the 52 greatest musicians, the architects of the Frequency Grid.
- **Betrayed by the Harmony Council**, stripped of power, and exiled underground, where they
  **mutated** and harnessed raw, chaotic sound.
- **Goal:** destroy the Frequency Grid and **free all sound**. They believe the Grid is a prison and
  the Council is corrupt; the only fix is to tear the whole system down and start over.
- **Method:** chaos and unpredictability. No single base (abandoned sound towers, underground
  studios, hacked radio stations). They weaponize sound gear (bass cannons, distortion rifles, sonic
  mines), hijack signals, and seize districts — attempting to take Ongaku **once every week**, so the
  war perpetually "resets."
- **Post-collapse:** **Hip-Hop** is the dominant genre of rebellion; they now *rule* much of the
  underground, distribute **Black Noise** (a sound-based perception drug), and hold no-go zones.

Full roster in `05-sick52-roster.md`.

## 🏛️ Harmony Council — The Overlords

**"One sound, one rhythm, one world."**

- Rulers of **Ongaku Prime**; enforce **absolute** music control via the **SBS**.
- Believe chaotic music caused past wars; consider all Syncopates dangerous anomalies.
- Control all major cities, Sound Towers, and music tech; can alter frequencies to crush rebellion;
  erase people from history.
- **Key members:** **Maestro Noctis** (unseen leader), **Grand Composer Veyra** (strict public face),
  **The Conductor** (elite enforcer of orchestral waves). Council also described as primarily
  **animal members, with some human & machine** (Season 3).

## 🏢 NexaGen Harmonics — The Corporate War Machine

**"Music is power. Power belongs to those who control it."**

- Cybernetic megacorp that **weaponizes music**; once worked under the Council, now pursues its own
  agenda: the ultimate music-based lifeform.
- **Harvested the DNA of the Sick 52** to mass-produce cyber-enhanced mutants and AI musicians.
- **Key figures:** **Lysander Coda** (ruthless CEO — "organic music is obsolete"), **Synth-09**
  (unstable "perfect musician" AI DJ), **Unit Omega** (mass-produced enforcer), and the secret
  **Project Harmonia** (a living god of music: fusion of tech, sound, and control).

## 🤡 The Komedians — Reality-Warping Invaders

**"Reality is just a setup. We are the punchline."**

- Interdimensional beings from **Planet Joke** who weaponize humor — they **rewrite worlds into
  jokes** rather than conquering them. Discovered Ongaku through an **XDF breach** and launched the
  **Laughing War** (Season 2).
- **Leader:** **The Grand Punchline**, plotting the **Eternal Laughtrack**. Foot soldiers: **Joke
  Warriors** (often former people twisted into living memes). Weapons: distortion cannons, joke-tech
  hallucinations, reality-erasing reversed-sound fields.

## ⚔️ The Rival — Sync's Personal Enemy

**"You were never meant to be the star. That's my role."**

- A former ally/competitor who rose in Sync's absence; the **Champion of Ongaku Prime**, the system's
  perfect weapon. Personal, not ideological — wants to prove Sync was never special.
- Candidate names from the source: **Vex, Riven, Sonus, Requiem, Drez.** (On the live site, **Drez**
  is used as the Mavericks' leader — see `CANON-NOTES.md`.)
