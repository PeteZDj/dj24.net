## Premise

On **Planet Ongaku**, music is not entertainment — it is **infrastructure and biology**. Sound
powers energy, architecture, travel, communication, and combat, and at the highest levels it can
reshape matter, space, and time. Children are trained in rhythm and sound-manipulation from birth.
The galaxy depends on Ongaku's cultural output "like a body depends on a heartbeat."

## The Frequency Grid

A planetary-scale system at Ongaku's center that:

- **Stabilizes reality** through sound alignment.
- **Hid Ongaku** from the rest of the galaxy (a cloaking function).
- Was **built by the original Sonic Order** (the 52 greatest musicians — later the Sick 52).

Its **collapse** during the First War (end of Season 1) is the hinge of the whole saga: it exposes
Ongaku to interplanetary forces and interdimensional entities (the Komedians) and escalates a
planetary struggle into an intergalactic war.

## The Single Beat System (SBS)

The **Harmony Council's** law of sound: a single, government-approved rhythm imposed on all of
Ongaku. Justified as "free, chaotic music caused the old wars; only controlled rhythm keeps peace."
In practice it censors genres, controls all Sound Towers, and erases dissidents from history.

## The Syncopate & XDF

- A **Syncopate** can "break the beat" — break, remix, and reshape sound waves, altering music and
  reality. This is **Harmonic Disruption**.
- **Sync** (the protagonist) is the awakening of this legend; he is the first DJ24 member naturally
  attuned to **Extra-Dimensional Frequencies (XDF)** — frequencies outside Ongaku's natural spectrum
  that touch parallel realities.
- Framing line: *"He doesn't glitch — he aligns."*

## The science (grounded model)

The lore deliberately grounds the magic in wave physics:

- **Universal Low-Frequency Oscillator (LFO):** a naturally occurring standing wave in Ongaku's
  planetary core/magnetosphere — analogous to Earth's **Schumann Resonance (~7.83 Hz)**. It acts as a
  planetary carrier wave that synchronizes all life ("**Resonant Synchronization**").
- **Resonant Synchronization:** people channel energy through sound and amplify their own biology;
  cities are built to resonate at specific frequencies.
- **Quantum Harmonics (XD Era):** once the LFO breaches into other dimensions, certain frequencies
  manipulate space and time — enabling rhythm-powered portals, matter-altering sound constructs, and
  a new galactic-scale energy source.

## Units of time

- **Soundwave Cycle (SWC):** the in-world year. **1 SWC ≈ 1.5 Earth years.**
- **10 SWC ≈ 15 Earth years** — the point where Sync's story begins.
- Eras are also spaced in **"lifecycles"** (e.g., the XD Era begins ~10 lifecycles after the HD Era).

## Dual-timeline design

The world is intentionally built so products can sit in two modes:

- **The Past (Low-Res):** the Golden Age of pure music combat — no guns, no tech, just rhythm duels.
- **The Future (High-Res / DJ24):** full cyberpunk — guns, AI, cybernetics, weaponized sound.

games map onto it.
