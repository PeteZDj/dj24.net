The 24 guardians = one per hour of the day. The live site groups them into **six four-member
squads**, ordered by tactical strength (command core → final failsafe). This is the **canonical
hour roster** used on the site; the source `the-dj-24.md` also contains earlier naming iterations
(see `CANON-NOTES.md`).

## The 54 reserves

Canonically DJ24 also keeps **54 reserve members** who rotate in. They are referenced but not yet
individually rostered — an open expansion slot.
