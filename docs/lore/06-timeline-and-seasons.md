## The Eras (the era ladder)

| Era | a.k.a. | Defining event | Mode |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Pre-Rhythm** | The Age of Noise | Raw, unstructured sound; discovery of the planetary **LFO** | — |
| **Low-Res Era** | The Age of Harmony | Golden Age of pure music combat; rhythm duels, dojos | The Past |
| **(Silence War)** | Transition | Rulers try to force one controlled beat; sound weaponized | Bridge |
| **HD Era** | The Age of Control | Scientifica contact, sound-tech, **Harmony Council + SBS**, betrayal of the 52 | The Future begins |
| **XD Era** | Extra-Dimensions | **Frequency Grid collapses**; XDF breaches; Komedians invade | Cyberpunk |
| **Post-XD / Galactic** | The Universe Expands | Sound warfare goes interstellar; Ongaku becomes a cosmic power | Galactic |

Time is measured in **Soundwave Cycles (SWC)**: 1 SWC ≈ 1.5 Earth years; the XD Era begins ~10
lifecycles after the HD Era. See `01-world-and-science.md`.

## The Seasons (the anime/story spine)

### Season 1 — "The Internal War"
Sync joins DJ24. The escalating war against the Sick 52 culminates in the **collapse of the Frequency
Grid**, exposing Ongaku to the galaxy. (Site `lore.md`.)

### Season 2 — "The Komedian Invasion"
*(~12–15 chapters; source 25.)* Six months after the collapse, the Council covers up the truth.
**Dr. Aulon** (Scientifica) warns that the **Komedians** are coming. Reality rips open; the first
city falls. **General 24 vs. Sync**, then the Komedians capture Sync and take him to **Planet Joke**
(the "Joke Arena," ruled by **The Grand Punchline**). Climax: the Komedians trigger the **Eternal
Laughtrack** frequency bomb; Sync taps his full XDF to counter it and resets Ongaku's soundscape.
Ending: the Komedians are banished, **DJ24 is fractured, the Harmonic Council is over, and Sync
becomes a wanderer.**
- Arc 1 "The Warning & The Cover-Up" (Ch 1–5)
- Arc 2 "The Laughing War" (Ch 6–10)
- Arc 3 "The Last Frequency" (Ch 11–15)

### Season 3 — "The AI War"
*(source 24.)* **General 24 + Billionaire X** ally with the **Classic Music AI** at the floating city
**Neon Nexus** to build an **AI DJ-warrior army** to exterminate the Sick 52 — the AI's price is
access to Ongaku's natural sound core. Meanwhile **Sync** wanders (Jazz Haven with Ninja, then the
**Rockers**) and slides into rage against the Sick 52, who grow stronger under a **new leader more
dangerous than Red Silence** (an open design slot). The AI gains autonomy and **demands sovereignty**;
the Harmony Council brands General 24 a rogue leader; Billionaire X convinces him to abandon the war
and leave Ongaku for the first time (visiting Ecomerica, Fabriqua, Chronos).

### Season 4 — "The Forgotten Soldiers"
*(source 24.)* DJ24 is fractured. **Sync becomes an anti-hero** hunting the worst Sick 52 remnants.
The **Harmony Council** leads the fight against the Sick 52. The **AI musicians rebel and form their
own sovereign nation.** Ends on the warning: **"Something worse than the Komedians is coming."**

## The First XD War

The umbrella event (source 9): the combined assault on Ongaku by the **Komedians and the Sick 52**
after the Grid falls — the moment the conflict stops being planetary and becomes interdimensional /
intergalactic.
