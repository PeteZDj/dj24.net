# Planet Ongaku — Universe Overview

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A breathtaking panoramic view of Planet Ongaku from space, a vibrant world with visible sonic wave patterns across its surface, glowing cities shaped like musical instruments, neon lights in purple and cyan, sci-fi anime style" -->
<!-- IMAGE: Replace with generated image → ![Planet Ongaku Overview](/images/universe/planet-ongaku.png) -->

**DJ24: War of Sound** is a large-scale sci-fi action universe where entire planets are structured around a single occupation to produce and export one main resource.

**[[Planet Ongaku]]** is the most culturally powerful world in the galaxy. Here, everyone is a musician, and music is infrastructure. Sound controls energy, architecture, travel, communication, combat, and reality itself. The galaxy depends on Ongaku's output like a body depends on a heartbeat.

Music is not optional — children are trained from birth in rhythm and sound manipulation.

## The Frequency Grid

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A massive glowing grid of energy beams made of visible sound waves, stretching across a planet's core, pulsing with golden and violet light, crackling with sonic energy, sci-fi concept art" -->

At Ongaku's center stood the **Frequency Grid** — a planetary system stabilizing reality through sound alignment. It hid Ongaku from the rest of the galaxy.

During the First War against the [[Sick 52]], the Frequency Grid collapsed. This exposed Ongaku to interplanetary forces, interdimensional entities (like [[The Komedians]]), and expanded the conflict from a planetary struggle into an intergalactic war.

The Grid was originally built by the 52 greatest musicians — the **Sonic Order** — who would later become the [[Sick 52]] after their betrayal by the [[Harmony Council]].

## The Myth of the Syncopate

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A silhouette of a young man standing in a vortex of broken musical notes and shattered sound waves, energy radiating from his hands, dramatic backlighting in cyan and magenta, anime key visual style" -->

Legend speaks of a **Syncopate** — a rare individual born with the power to "break the beat" in a society governed by the [[Harmony Council]]'s rigid **Single Beat System (SBS)**.

The Syncopate possesses **Harmonic Disruption**: the ability to break, remix, and reshape sound waves, altering music and reality.

[[Sync]], the protagonist, is the awakening of this legend. His emergence changes everything — from the internal politics of [[DJ24]] to the interdimensional wars that follow.

---

## The Single Beat System (SBS)

The SBS is the [[Harmony Council]]'s iron law: all music on Ongaku must conform to a single approved rhythmic pattern. Deviation is punished by **sonic erasure** — having your sound signature wiped from existence.

The SBS was enacted after the chaos of the early music wars, but it became a tool of authoritarian control. The [[Sick 52]] and the [[Resonant Sons]] both oppose it, though for different reasons.

## Genre Megacities

Planet Ongaku is divided into genre-based megacities, each with distinct architecture, culture, and combat styles:

- **Rock District** — Industrial, raw, guitar-shaped towers
- **Electronic Sector** — Neon-lit, pulsing with synthesized beats
- **Hip-Hop Underground** — Where the [[Sick 52]] built their rebel strongholds
- **Classical Heights** — The [[Harmony Council]]'s seat of power
- **Jazz Quarter** — Free-form, improvised, where [[Sync]] finds his path in Season 3
