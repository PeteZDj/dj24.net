# Seasons — Story Arcs

The story of [[DJ24]]: War of Sound unfolds across four major seasons, each escalating the conflict.

---

## Season 1: The Internal War

[[Sync]] joins [[DJ24]]. The escalating war against the [[Sick 52]] leads to the collapse of the [[Frequency Grid]] and exposes [[Planet Ongaku]] to the galaxy.

**Key Events:**
- Sync discovers his [[Syncopate]] abilities
- [[DJ24]] internal factions form: [[General 24]]'s Harmonic Order, [[Drez]]'s Mavericks, and the Resonant Sons
- The [[Frequency Grid]] collapses in the climactic battle

---

## Season 2: The Komedian Invasion

The breakdown of the [[Frequency Grid]] invites [[The Komedians]]. [[Sync]] is captured and taken to Planet Joke. The war becomes an interdimensional survival fight.

**Key Events:**
- [[The Komedians]] breach reality through XDF
- Cities are rewritten into nightmares of absurdity
- [[DJ24]] and [[Sick 52]] form a temporary, uneasy alliance

---

## Season 3: The AI War

[[General 24]], disillusioned by endless conflict, allies with [[NexaGen Harmonics]]'s AI musicians to create an unstoppable synthetic army to crush the [[Sick 52]].

[[Sync]], feeling lost, wanders and explores freeform Jazz and rebel Rockers.

**Key Events:**
- General 24 embraces AI warfare
- Sync finds his musical identity through Jazz
- The [[Sick 52]] grows stronger in the underground

---

## Season 4: The Forgotten Soldiers

[[General 24]] explores intergalactic politics. [[Sync]] acts as an anti-hero hunting the worst [[Sick 52]] remnants. [[NexaGen Harmonics]]'s AI army rebels, demanding sovereignty. A new cosmic threat looms.

**Key Events:**
- DJ24 fractures completely
- NexaGen's AI becomes sentient and demands freedom
- A new cosmic threat emerges on the galactic stage
