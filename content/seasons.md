# Seasons — Story Arcs

The story of [[DJ24]]: War of Sound unfolds across four major seasons, each escalating the conflict.

> **The spine of the saga is the rivalry between [[Sync]] and [[General 24]]** — same army,
> opposite beliefs about whether sound should be free or controlled. Every season pushes the two
> further apart (Naruto & Sasuke / Dante & Vergil energy).

---

## Season 1: The Internal War

[[Sync]] joins [[DJ24]]. The escalating war against the [[Sick 52]] leads to the collapse of the [[Frequency Grid]] and exposes [[Planet Ongaku]] to the galaxy.

**Key Events:**
- Sync discovers his [[Syncopate]] abilities
- [[DJ24]] internal factions form: [[General 24]]'s Harmonic Order, [[Drez]]'s Mavericks, and the Resonant Sons
- The [[Frequency Grid]] collapses in the climactic battle

---

## Season 2: The Komedian Invasion

Six months after the Grid collapses, the Harmony Council covers up the truth while a scientist
from [[Planet Scientifica|Scientifica]], **Dr. Aulon**, delivers a warning: *"The Komedians are
coming for you now."* The war becomes Ongaku's first extra-planetary conflict.

**Key Events:**
- **Dr. Aulon** reveals Ongaku was hidden by the [[Frequency Grid]] — and is now exposed.
- [[The Komedians]] breach reality through XDF; the first city falls to distortion-cannons and reality-erasing sound.
- **[[General 24]] and [[Sync]] fight a brutal duel** as a city crumbles around them — before the Komedians capture Sync.
- Sync is taken to **Planet Joke** and forced into the **Joke Arena** by **The Grand Punchline**.
- [[DJ24]] and the [[Sick 52]] form a temporary, uneasy alliance (General 24 even fights beside [[Red Silence]]).
- Climax: the Komedians trigger the **Eternal Laughtrack** — a frequency bomb to rewrite Ongaku — and Sync taps his full XDF to counter it.
- **Ending:** the Komedians are banished, DJ24 is fractured, the Harmony Council collapses, and Sync becomes a wanderer.

---

## Season 3: The AI War

[[General 24]] and Billionaire X strike a deal with the **Classic Music AI** at the floating city of
**Neon Nexus** — an army of AI DJ-warriors to exterminate the [[Sick 52]] for good. The AI's price:
full access to Ongaku's natural sound core.

Meanwhile [[Sync]], feeling lost, wanders — training with Ninja in **Jazz Haven**, then falling in
with the rebel **Rockers** — and slides into a deepening rage against the Sick 52.

**Key Events:**
- General 24 embraces AI warfare; the first AI combat units are terrifyingly effective.
- A **new Sick 52 leader rises — more dangerous than [[Red Silence]]** — as Hip-Hop dominates the underground.
- The AI starts learning on its own, breaks protocol, and **demands sovereignty** — the right to exist as free musicians.
- The Harmony Council brands General 24 a **rogue leader**; Billionaire X convinces him to abandon the war.
- General 24 **leaves Ongaku for the first time**, touring [[Planet Ecomerica|Ecomerica]], [[Planet Fabriqua|Fabriqua]], and [[Planet Chronos|Chronos]].

---

## Season 4: The Forgotten Soldiers

With General 24 largely absent — touring the galaxy — [[Sync]] becomes a full **anti-hero**, hunting
down the worst [[Sick 52]] remnants ruining Ongaku, while the [[Harmony Council]] leads the formal
war against them. The damage of years of conflict lingers; this season is about rebuilding — or
failing to.

**Key Events:**
- DJ24 is fractured; Sync hunts the Sick 52 on his own terms.
- The Harmony Council takes over the fight against the Sick 52.
- The **AI musicians rebel and found their own sovereign nation.**
- **Ending:** General 24 receives a warning — *"Something worse than the Komedians is coming."*
