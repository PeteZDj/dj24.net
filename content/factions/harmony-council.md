# Harmony Council — The Overlords

The rulers of [[Planet Ongaku|Ongaku Prime]] who enforce absolute control over music via the **Single Beat System (SBS)**. They view chaotic music as the root of past wars that nearly destroyed the planet. Their motto: *"One sound, one rhythm, one world."*

The Council occupies the **Golden Conservatory**, a massive floating citadel above the capital city of [[Classic City]]. From within its gilded halls, they broadcast the SBS signal that keeps every citizen's rhythm locked to the approved 120 BPM standard. Deviation is not merely illegal — it is considered a disease to be cured, by force if necessary.

---

## History

The Harmony Council was not always a dictatorship. It was originally formed during the **Era of Cacophony**, a devastating period in which competing Genre Nations weaponized music against each other. Billions died in the **Frequency Wars** — conflicts fought with sonic weapons that leveled cities and shattered continents.

In the aftermath, the surviving nations formed the Council as a peacekeeping body. Their mandate was simple: **prevent another Frequency War at any cost**. For a time, they succeeded. They commissioned the [[Frequency Grid]] — a planet-wide sound regulation system designed by the man who would become [[Red Silence]] — and used it to stabilize Ongaku's fractured atmosphere.

But power corrupts. Over the centuries, the Council transformed from a peace-keeping body into an authoritarian regime. They began restricting genres, outlawing improvisation, and finally imposing the Single Beat System — a single approved rhythm that all citizens must follow. They rebranded their control as "Harmony" and their oppression as "Peace."

The [[Sick 52]] were once the Council's most trusted builders. They designed the Grid, the Conservatory, and the sonic infrastructure that makes Ongaku function. When the Council realized that these same builders could also *dismantle* everything they'd built, they moved to eliminate them. The betrayal was swift, brutal, and incomplete — and it birthed the most dangerous rebel faction in Ongaku's history.

---

## The Single Beat System

The SBS mandates that all music on Ongaku must conform to a single approved rhythmic pattern — currently locked at **120 BPM, 4/4 time, C-major only**. Deviation is punished by **sonic erasure** — having your sound signature wiped from existence.

The system works through the [[Frequency Grid]], which broadcasts the approved rhythm into every building, street, and public space. Citizens who listen to unapproved music experience physical discomfort — headaches, nausea, disorientation — conditioned responses implanted through years of exposure to the Grid's carrier signal.

- **Level 1 Violation** — Playing an unapproved tempo. Punishment: public correction and re-education.
- **Level 2 Violation** — Performing an unapproved genre. Punishment: instrument confiscation and 6 months in a Silence Chamber.
- **Level 3 Violation** — Composing original music. Punishment: permanent sonic erasure.

## Council Members

### Maestro Noctis — *The Unseen Hand*

![Maestro Noctis](/images/harmony-council/maestro-noctis.png)

**Role:** Supreme Arbiter of Sound | **Status:** Active (Hidden)

The oldest living being on Ongaku. No citizen has seen his face in over 300 years. He communicates only through resonance — a low, golden hum that transmits his will directly into the minds of the other Council members. Some believe he is no longer human; that he merged with the Frequency Grid itself during the Cacophony Era and now *is* the system.

He made the decision to betray the Sick 52. He signed the order to impose the SBS. Every horror the Council has committed traces back to his golden hum. He is not evil in the traditional sense — he genuinely believes that music must be controlled or it will destroy everything. His tragedy is that he may be right.

---

### Grand Composer Veyra — *The Iron Baton*

![Grand Composer Veyra](/images/harmony-council/grand-composer-veyra.png)

**Role:** Public Face of the Council | **Status:** Active

The most visible member of the Harmony Council. She is the one who delivers speeches, signs execution orders, and stands before the cameras while Noctis hides in the shadows. She is beautiful, eloquent, and utterly merciless.

She was a child prodigy who won the **Grand Composition Tournament** at age 9 — the last tournament ever held before the SBS banned original composition. The irony is not lost on her, but she believes the sacrifice was necessary. "I gave up my art so that Ongaku could survive," she says. "Everyone else can do the same."

She is not to be confused with [[Veyra|Veyra "Sonic Knight"]] of [[DJ24]], who is a distant relative and ideological opposite.

---

### The Conductor — *The Golden Fist*

![The Conductor](/images/harmony-council/the-conductor.png)

**Role:** Chief Enforcer | **Status:** Active

The Council's executioner. Where Grand Composer Veyra is the voice, The Conductor is the fist. He leads the **Harmonic Guard** — the Council's elite military force — and personally oversees all Level 3 violations.

He was once a concert conductor who could command an entire orchestra telepathically. The Council recognized his talent and "promoted" him — which meant replacing his empathy with conditioning and his baton with a weapon. He remembers what music felt like before the SBS, and he hates himself for what he's become. But he follows orders. Always.

---

### Arbiter Shen — *The Gavel*

![Arbiter Shen](/images/harmony-council/arbiter-shen.png)

**Role:** Chief Judge of Sound Law | **Status:** Active

Every sonic erasure on Ongaku passes through Arbiter Shen's desk. He has signed more death warrants than any other single being in the planet's history, and he does it with the same expression he uses to eat breakfast — bored indifference.

He views music as a legal construct, not an art form. To him, a melody is a contract and a rhythm is a regulation. He has never hummed a tune, never tapped his foot, never felt the swell of a chord. He is the perfect bureaucrat: efficient, incorruptible, and completely empty inside.

---

### Warden Forte — *The Silence Keeper*

![Warden Forte](/images/harmony-council/warden-forte.png)

**Role:** Director of Silence Chambers | **Status:** Active

The man who runs the prisons. The Silence Chambers are the Council's most feared punishment facility — soundproofed cells where prisoners are kept in absolute silence for months or years. The experience drives most inmates permanently insane.

Forte was a drill sergeant who discovered he enjoyed the quiet. Not in a peaceful way — in the way a predator enjoys a cornered prey. He designed improvements to the Chambers that increased their effectiveness by 400%. He considers this his life's greatest achievement.

---

### Minister Lyra — *The Smiling Knife*

![Minister Lyra](/images/harmony-council/minister-lyra.png)

**Role:** Head of Propaganda & Public Relations | **Status:** Active

The most dangerous member of the Council, because she makes people *want* to obey. While others rule through fear, Lyra rules through charm. She is the architect of the **"Harmony Is Freedom"** campaign that convinced an entire generation that the SBS was a gift, not a chain.

She personally designs the Council's public messaging, educational curriculum, and cultural programming. Every child on Ongaku grows up singing the songs she wrote. Every advertisement uses rhythms she approved. She has shaped the cultural consciousness of an entire planet, and she did it with a smile.

---

### Auditor Kane — *The Frequency Inspector*

![Auditor Kane](/images/harmony-council/auditor-kane.png)

**Role:** Chief of the Frequency Compliance Bureau | **Status:** Active

The man who listens. Kane runs the **Frequency Compliance Bureau (FCB)** — a vast surveillance network that monitors every sound on Ongaku in real-time. Every conversation, every hummed melody, every tapped rhythm is logged, analyzed, and flagged.

He was a sound engineer who discovered that certain frequencies could reveal emotional states. The Council hired him to build a system that could detect dissent through sound alone. He succeeded. Now he sits in a white room, surrounded by screens, listening to an entire planet whisper its secrets. He has not left the room in 14 years.

## Power Assessment

> ⚠️ **Battle Rating: EXTREMELY WEAK**
>
> The Harmony Council members are *politicians, bureaucrats, and administrators* — not warriors. Their power comes from the systems they control, not from personal combat ability. In a direct fight against even the lowest-ranked DJ24 member, any Council member would be annihilated. Their true danger lies in the institutions, armies, and infrastructure they command.

## Role in the Story

The Harmony Council is the hidden puppeteer behind many of the universe's conflicts. They created the conditions that birthed the [[Sick 52]], and their rigid control system is what [[Sync]] — the [[Syncopate]] — is destined to break.

In **[[Season 1|Season 1: The Internal War]]**, the Council operates in the background, using [[DJ24]] as their proxy army against the [[Sick 52]] while maintaining deniability. By **Season 2**, their direct involvement becomes undeniable as they attempt to consolidate power through the **Frequency Grid Override** — a plan to permanently lock the Grid to their will.

Their ultimate goal is the **Final Composition** — a single, eternal note that would freeze all sound on Ongaku in a state of permanent "harmony." To the Council, this is paradise. To everyone else, it is the death of music.
