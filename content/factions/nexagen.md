# NexaGen Harmonics — The Corporate War Machine

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A massive corporate cyberpunk skyscraper shaped like a tuning fork, with digital holographic advertisements for AI musicians, neon cyan and teal lighting, dark futuristic cityscape, corporate dystopia anime style" -->

A cybernetic megacorporation that weaponized music. They harvest DNA, mass-produce AI artists, and secretly scheme to create the ultimate music-based lifeform through **Project Harmonia**.

They cloned the [[Sick 52]] survivors, producing one new mutant every week — industrializing war itself.

Their motto: *"Music is power. Power belongs to those who control it."*

---

## Operations

- **DNA Harvesting** — Kidnapping [[Sick 52]] survivors to extract their sonic mutations
- **AI Music Production** — Creating synthetic musicians to replace organic artists
- **Project Harmonia** — The forbidden weapon: a fusion of human and machine sound
- **Clone Army** — Mass-producing Sick 52 clones as disposable soldiers

---

## Key Members

- **Lysander Coda** — Ruthless CEO who believes organic music is obsolete.
- **Synth-09** — Unstable AI DJ masterpiece — the "perfect musician." Their greatest creation and biggest threat.
- **Project Harmonia** — The forbidden weapon — a fusion of human and machine sound.

---

## Role in the Story

NexaGen operates in the shadows during [[Season 1|Season 1]] and [[Season 2|Season 2]], but becomes a major player in **[[Season 3|Season 3: The AI War]]** when [[General 24]] allies with their technology.

By **[[Season 4|Season 4: The Forgotten Soldiers]]**, NexaGen's AI army rebels, demanding sovereignty — creating a new existential threat.
