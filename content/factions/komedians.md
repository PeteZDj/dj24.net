# The Komedians — The Reality-Warping Invaders

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "Bizarre interdimensional clown-like entities emerging from a laughing portal, reality around them warping into cartoon-like distortions, surreal and unsettling, vibrant pink and purple chaos, anime horror comedy style" -->

Interdimensional beings from **Planet Joke** who weaponize humor. They don't conquer worlds — they **rewrite them into jokes**. Cities bend into caricatures, people become punchlines, and sound becomes mockery.

Their arrival through **Extra-Dimensional Frequencies (XDF)** changed the war from planetary to cosmic.

Their motto: *"Reality is just a setup. We are the punchline."*

---

## Abilities

- **Reality Rewriting** — Transform environments into absurd, joke-like distortions
- **XDF Manipulation** — Travel between dimensions using Extra-Dimensional Frequencies
- **The Laughing War** — Their invasion strategy: break minds through weaponized absurdity
- **The Eternal Laughtrack** — Their ultimate goal: rewrite all of reality into an endless comedy

---

## Key Members

- **The Grand Punchline** — Ruling power plotting the Eternal Laughtrack.
- **Joke Warriors** — Reality-bending soldiers of farce.

---

## Role in the Story

The Komedians arrive in **[[Season 2|Season 2: The Komedian Invasion]]** after the [[Frequency Grid]]'s collapse creates an XDF breach. [[Sync]] is captured and taken to Planet Joke, where the war becomes an interdimensional survival fight.

Their invasion forces [[DJ24]] and the [[Sick 52]] into an uneasy temporary alliance — the only time these bitter enemies fight on the same side.
