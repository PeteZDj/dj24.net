# DJ24: The Sick 52

> *"Fifty-two names. One deck. Deal them all."*

**DJ24: The Sick 52** is the game built directly on the [[The Sick Deck|Deck of 52]] system. It
takes the open-world **"most-wanted" bounty loop** (made famous by *Mercenaries: Playground of
Destruction*) and rebuilds it around **sound-combat**: you play [[DJ24]], hunting the
[[Sick 52]] one card at a time across the cities of [[Planet Ongaku]].

Browse the live systems: the **[Sick Deck](/sick-deck)** board · the **[Missions](/missions)**
bounty list · the full **[Sick 52](/sick52)** roster.

---

## The Core Loop

1. **Pick a House.** The 52 enemies are split into four [[The Sick Deck|houses]] (suits).
2. **Work the ladder.** In each house you hunt from the **10 → Ace**: clear field targets to earn
   intel, which unlocks the face cards, then the **Ace** boss.
3. **Field the right branch.** You deploy one of the four [[DJ24]] **service branches** — each
   counters one house.
4. **Liberate the city.** Each card holds territory in a city; beating it loosens the
   [[Harmony Council]]'s / Sick 52's grip and opens the next district.
5. **Climb to the Final Drop.** All roads end at **A♠ — [Final Drop](/sick52/final-drop)**.

## Your Forces — The Four DJ24 Branches

This is the **Army / Navy / Airforce / Space Force** axis — and it belongs to **DJ24**, not the
Sick 52. It is a *combat-doctrine* sort that sits beside the six strength
[[DJ24 Roster|squads]]: the squads say *who outranks who*; the branches say *what they're good at*
and *which house they counter*.

| Branch | Domain | Counters | Notable Guardians |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🛰️ **Space Force** | Reality, Time & Command | ♠ Spades | [[General 24]], [[Sync]], 4Serj, d.O.P, WBoy, AfroG |
| 🪖 **Army** | Ground Assault & Percussion | ♣ Clubs | Striker, Nova, King J, Distort, Mr Genge, Breakline |
| ⚓ **Navy** | Sub-Bass, Flow & Pressure | ♦ Diamonds | MasterBass, LiquidB, SubZ, Sidechain, Crossfade, Backspin |
| ✈️ **Airforce** | Speed, Tempo & the Mind | ♥ Hearts | Molly, Ninja Nagazaki, Afterimage, Moombah, Maya, GhostLoop |

Each branch fields **6 guardians** (24 total). You bring a squad of branch-matched guardians into a
city and swap doctrine on the fly.

## Combat — Bass vs Treble

Sound-combat runs on two stances, mirroring the lore's [[Frequency Grid|low/high frequency]] split:

- **BASS stance** — heavy, slow, area control. Knockback, zoning, armor. Best against swarms and
  the ♣ Clubs / ♦ Diamonds brawlers.
- **TREBLE stance** — fast, precise, single-target. Combos, parries, crits. Best against the
  ♥ Hearts mind-twisters and evasive ♠ Spades.

You **drop** between the two (a "transition") to chain combos — landing a transition *on the beat*
of the level's track multiplies damage. This is where [[Sync]]'s alignment power and Crossfade's
mix-engineering become literal mechanics.

## Bodyguards & the Card Hierarchy

Every card above a **2** travels with **lower cards as bodyguards**:

- Defeating **pip cards (10 → 2)** strips an **Ace's** guard and reveals its location.
- **Face cards (J/Q/K)** are mini-bosses — beat them to weaken the Ace's buffs.
- **Aces** only spawn once their house is cleared down to them.

So the "hunt 10 → Ace" order isn't just flavor — it's the unlock graph.

## Missions & Bounties

Two mission types feed the loop. See the full board on the **[Missions](/missions)** page.

- **Story Missions** — the hand-authored campaign spine (First Contact → the Final Drop), pulling in
  the [[Seasons|Komedian Invasion]] and the [[Sync]] vs [[General 24]] rivalry.
- **Bounties** — one repeatable contract per card (52 total), each tied to a **city**, a
  **difficulty** (from card strength) and a **reward** (Aces pay the most).

## City-Lock — Where the Cards Live

The hunt plays out across the districts of [[Planet Ongaku]]. Each house holds turf:

| House | Home Turf |
|---|---|
| ♠ Spades — High Command | Intro: The Dream · Classic City · Cloud City |
| ♣ Clubs — War Conductors | Rock City · Urban City |
| ♦ Diamonds — Elemental Corps | Electric City · Blue City |
| ♥ Hearts — Dissonant Choir | Pop City · Rose City · Joke City · Hall of Laughter |

Clearing a house's cards **unlocks** its cities and weakens the enemy's hold on the
[[Frequency Grid]].

---

## The Joker

The **Joker** is not one of the 52. It is the [[The Komedians|Komedian]] wildcard — an
extra-dimensional incursion from [[The Galaxy|Planet Joke]]. It ignores houses, branches and
bodyguards, and appears during the [[Seasons|Komedian Invasion]] arc when [[Sync]] is captured.

---

## Why This Game

Of the [[Games|five games]] in the universe, **DJ24: The Sick 52** is the one the Deck of 52 was
made for: it already stars the Sick 52 by name, sits in the explosive **Mid-XD Era**, and frames
the whole roster as targets. The deck gives it a ready-made progression curve, the branches give it
party-building, and the cities give it an open world.
