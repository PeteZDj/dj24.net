# The Frequency Grid

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A massive glowing grid of energy beams made of visible sound waves, stretching across a planet's core, pulsing with golden and violet light, crackling with sonic energy, dark underground cyberpunk concept art" -->

The **Frequency Grid** is the single most important structure on [[Planet Ongaku]]. A planetary system stabilizing reality through sound alignment, it hid Ongaku from the rest of the galaxy for centuries.

## Origins

The Grid was originally built by the **[[Sonic Order]]** — the 52 greatest musicians on [[Planet Ongaku]]. These masters pooled their combined frequency powers to create a living shield of harmonized sound. Each of the 52 nodes of the Grid required constant maintenance by one of the masters.

## Structure

The Grid exists as a web of interlocking standing waves spread across and beneath the planet's surface. At its peak:

- **52 primary nodes** — each maintained by a [[Sonic Order]] master
- **Planetary cloaking** — hid Ongaku from interstellar detection
- **Reality stabilization** — prevented dimensional rifts
- **Energy generation** — powered all of Ongaku's [[Genre Megacities]]
- **Communication backbone** — enabled instant sonic messaging across the planet

## The Collapse

During the First War between [[DJ24]] and the [[Sick 52]], the Frequency Grid catastrophically collapsed. This had galaxy-altering consequences:

1. **Exposure** — [[Planet Ongaku]] was suddenly visible to the galaxy
2. **Interdimensional breaches** — [[The Komedians]] and other entities gained entry
3. **Power loss** — many [[Genre Megacities]] lost their primary energy source
4. **Reality instability** — zones of the planet began experiencing temporal and spatial anomalies

The collapse escalated the conflict from a planetary civil war into an intergalactic crisis.
