# Combat Styles of Ongaku

> *"Rhythm is a weapon. The beat is your battlefield."*

On [[Planet Ongaku]], every race fights differently. Combat is driven by **sound manipulation**, and each species has evolved unique approaches to weaponizing frequency, rhythm, and resonance.

---

## 🟢 Humans (Harmonids) — Versatile Masters of Sound Warfare

> *"Rhythm is a weapon. We control it."*

Humans are the most **adaptable** fighters, balancing physical skill, cybernetic upgrades, and strategic mastery over sound.

### Combat Philosophy
- **Structured Sound Combat** — Deliberate, rehearsed techniques, like classical musicians following sheet music
- **Tactical Beats** — Manipulate tempo, syncopation, and counter-rhythm to gain advantage
- **Cybernetic Enhancements** — Some augment vocal cords, hands, or hearing

### Signature Techniques

| Technique | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Beat-Sync Strikes** | Multiple attacks in precise rhythm, overwhelming with speed and accuracy |
| **Tempo Reversal** | Shifts tempo mid-fight, causing enemies to miss or stutter |
| **Sonic Disruption** | Mid-frequency pulses that distort enemy perception |
| **Turntable Blade** | Circular sonic wave that spins like vinyl scratching, cutting through the battlefield |
| **Acoustic Shield** | Defensive wall of compressed sound, blocking attacks |

### Fighting Schools

| School | Style |
|---|---|
| **[[DJ24]] Members** | Structured, elite, military-grade fighters |
| **Street Performers** | Wild, unpredictable hip-hop brawlers with freeform styles |
| **Resonant Sons** | Traditionalists focused on pure, raw sound |

---

## 🦁 Animals (Sonari) — Natural Warriors of Sound Instinct

> *"The beat was in us before we could even speak."*

The Sonari evolved with music as part of their **biology**. They don't use structured techniques — they flow with sound instinctively.

### Combat Philosophy
- **Instinct-Based Fighting** — React to vibrations, frequencies, and natural rhythms
- **Biological Sound Manipulation** — Create sound directly from their bodies, no tech needed
- **Species-Specific Advantages** — Speed (felines), flight (birds), raw power (beasts)

### Signature Techniques

| Technique | Species | Effect |
|---|---|---|
| **Falcon Pulse** | Birds | High-frequency shrieks that paralyze or disorient |
| **Serpent Whisper** | Reptiles | Low-frequency rumbles that hypnotize enemies |
| **Rhythm Dash** | Felines | Perfect sync with the beat — nearly untouchable |
| **Drumquake** | Large beasts | Ground-slamming shockwaves traveling through the battlefield |
| **Howling Echo** | Pack wolves | Layered sound amplification for combined attacks |

### Fighting Clans

| Clan | Specialty |
|---|---|
| **The Jungle Drummers** | Percussive attacks, deep bass concussive force |
| **The Winged Harmonists** | Frequency-based aerial combat, unpredictable diving |
| **The Echo Pack** | Synchronized team fighting, combined sound attacks |

---

## 🤖 Machines (Mechavibes) — AI-Driven Fighters

> *"You improvise. We calculate."*

The Mechavibes are AI-driven fighters capable of processing sound combat in real-time, making them **almost impossible to trick**.

### Combat Philosophy
- **Calculated & Precise** — Analyze patterns and predict every move, no emotional reactions
- **Data-Driven Adaptation** — Download new techniques mid-fight based on enemy weaknesses
- **Digital Sound Manipulation** — Synthetic waves and cybernetic distortion

### Signature Techniques

| Technique | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Glitch Step** | Shifts BPM instantly, appearing to teleport |
| **Resonance Hacking** | Corrupts enemy soundwaves, breaking their rhythm |
| **Auto-Tune Armor** | Adjusts own frequency to cancel attacks |
| **Overclock Surge** | Near-instantaneous reaction time (temporary) |
| **Delay Feedback Loop** | Creates echoed afterimages to confuse opponents |

### Machine Factions

| Faction | Philosophy |
|---|---|
| **The Synth Lords** | Perfectly replicate human styles — remove all emotion |
| **The Glitchborn** | Rogue AIs fighting with unpredictability and broken rhythms |
| **The Silent Processors** | Weaponize complete silence — the deadliest of all |

## Hybrid Combat

When [[Races and Classes|races interbreed or merge]], hybrid combat styles emerge:

| Hybrid | Combat Style |
|---|---|
| **Human + Animal** | Beast-mode transformations with tactical awareness |
| **Animal + Machine** | Metal Gear-style walkers, biomech cavalry |
| **Machine + Human** | Gundam-style mech warriors with human intuition |
| **Full Hybrid** | Unpredictable — combines all three combat philosophies |
