# Classic City

![Classic City Logo](/images/logos/logo_classic_city.png)

## Overview
Stone castles shaped like instruments. Cathedrals resemble grand violins. Clock towers ring in harmonic intervals.

## Atmosphere and Aesthetic
* **Era:** Gold
* **Visuals:** Gold, warm brown, parchment ivory, medieval, violin and harp shapes
