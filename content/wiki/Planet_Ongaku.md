# The Main Cities of Ongaku

Planet Ongaku is a living musical tapestry, where cities are not just locations, but physical manifestations of musical eras, philosophies, and emotional frequencies. Each city is a cornerstone of the [[Frequency Grid]], anchoring the reality of the planet through its unique soundscape.

## Intro The Dream
The birthplace of the universe. Water floats in midair. Cities exist as reflections in liquid mirrors. The sky is not a sky — it’s a cosmic ocean ceiling.
