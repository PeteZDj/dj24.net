# The Eras of Ongaku

> *"A long time ago, in a galaxy far, far away…"*

The history of [[Planet Ongaku]] spans multiple ages, each defined by scientific discovery, societal shifts, and the evolving relationship between sound and civilization.

---

## Timeline of Eras

| # | Era | Designation | Key Event |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | **Pre-Rhythm (PR)** | Before Sound | The silence before music. Life existed, but without organized frequency. |
| 1 | **Low-Res Era (LR)** | Before Control | Golden Age of Music Battles. No guns, pure artistic mastery. |
| 2 | **High-Res Definition Era (HD)** | After Dominion | Tech advancements, weapons, rebellion against musical oppression. |

## Pre-Rhythm (PR) — The Silence Before

Before organized sound, life on Ongaku existed in a state of vibrational chaos. All three [[Races and Classes|races]] — Humans, Animals, and Machines — could not communicate across species boundaries.

### The Discovery of the LFO

The transition from PR to the Low-Res Era was triggered by the discovery of the **Low Frequency Oscillator (LFO)** — a naturally occurring wave emanating from the planet's core.

**The physics:** Ongaku's molten core contains dense deposits of **resonite**, a crystalline mineral that vibrates at sub-harmonic frequencies. When tectonic activity pushed resonite deposits closer to the surface, the resulting LFO wave created a **universal carrier signal** — a base frequency that all living things could synchronize to.

This LFO allowed:
- **Humans** to perceive animal vocalizations as structured communication
- **Animals** to recognize human-made rhythms as meaningful signals
- **Early proto-Machines** (natural silicon formations) to oscillate in response to organic sound

For the first time, all life on Ongaku shared a **common frequency** — the birth of music as universal language.

## Low-Res Era (LR) — The Golden Age

The Low-Res Era is defined by **pure artistic mastery**. No weapons, no tech-amplified combat — only raw sound skill determined a warrior's power.

### Key Characteristics
- Music battles fought with acoustic instruments and vocal projection
- The [[Frequency Grid]] is stable and accessible to all
- Genre territories begin to form — the seeds of the [[Genre Megacities]]
- The [[Harmony Council]] first rises as a peacekeeping body
- **No firearms or energy weapons** — combat relies on sound itself

### The Era of [[DJ Battle (Game)|DJ Battle]]
The first game in the franchise, **DJ Battle**, is set during this era. The player begins their journey in the final decades of the Low-Res Era, just before the transition to HD.

---

## The LR → HD Transition

The shift from Low-Res to High-Res was driven by **anomalous frequency observations**:

1. **Strange harmonics** — Scientists detected frequencies outside the known spectrum, emanating from beyond Ongaku's atmosphere
2. **The Visitor** — A foreigner from **Planet Scientifica** arrived, bearing advanced frequency technology. This visit was classified and kept secret by the [[Harmony Council]]
3. **Resonance Amplification** — The Scientifica technology allowed artificial amplification of the LFO, dramatically increasing the power of sound manipulation
4. **Weaponization** — With amplified frequencies came the ability to create **sonic weapons**, energy disruptors, and frequency barriers

The HD Era began the moment sound became a weapon of war, not just art.

---

## High-Res Definition Era (HD) — After Dominion

Technology transforms Ongaku. Sound amplification creates new powers — and new dangers.

### Key Characteristics
- Cybernetic sound augmentation becomes common
- [[NexaGen Harmonics]] rises as a corporate power
- The [[Sick 52]] are exiled and mutated
- Genre religions formalize into political structures
- Warfare now includes frequency cannons, sonic disruption fields, and resonance manipulation

---

## The HD → XD Transition

The Extra-Dimensions Era begins when Ongaku's frequency experiments accidentally **punch through dimensional barriers**:

1. **Frequency Overload** — HD-era amplification pushed the planet's resonite deposits to critical resonance
2. **The Dimensional Crack** — A frequency experiment tore open a gateway to extra-dimensional space
3. **First Contact** — Ongaku discovers it is not alone — other musical civilizations exist across the galaxy
4. **Galactic Expansion** — Sound travel networks form between worlds

---

## Extra-Dimensions (XD) — The Space Era

The far future of Ongaku, where music has expanded into intergalactic civilizations, advanced AI, and cosmic warfare.

### Key Characteristics
- **[[DJ24]]** operates as Ongaku's private super army, projecting power across galaxies
- Interplanetary travel via frequency tunnels
- AI civilizations have become fully autonomous
- The war of sound is now a **multi-world conflict**

### Setting of [[DJ24 (Game)]]
The main DJ24 game is set **10 lifecycles** (Ongaku's unit of years) after the start of the XD Era.

## Ongaku Time Units

| Unit | Earth Equivalent | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Beat** | ~1 second | Base unit of time |
| **Bar** | ~4 minutes (240 beats) | Common measurement |
| **Set** | ~1 day | One full rotation of Ongaku |
| **Track** | ~1 month (30 sets) | A lunar cycle |
| **Lifecycle** | ~1 year (12 tracks) | One orbital period |
