# The Single Beat System (SBS)

The **Single Beat System** is the [[Harmony Council]]'s iron law: all music on [[Planet Ongaku]] must conform to a single approved rhythmic pattern.

## Enforcement

Deviation from the SBS is punished by **sonic erasure** — having your sound signature wiped from existence. Without a sound signature on Ongaku, a person effectively becomes a ghost — unable to interact with sound-based infrastructure, buildings, or even basic communication.

## History

The SBS was enacted after the chaos of the early music wars, when unrestricted genre warfare nearly tore the planet apart. The [[Harmony Council]] framed it as necessary stability. Over time, it became a tool of authoritarian control.

## Opposition

Two major factions oppose the SBS:

- **The [[Sick 52]]** — who believe music must evolve through mutation and experimentation
- **The [[Resonant Sons]]** — a faction within [[DJ24]] who believe in freedom of musical expression

The [[Syncopate]] prophecy represents the ultimate threat to the SBS — a being who can break the beat itself.
