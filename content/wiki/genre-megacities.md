# Genre Megacities

[[Planet Ongaku]] is divided into **genre-based megacities**, each with distinct architecture, culture, combat styles, and religious identity. Each city is a physical manifestation of its genre religion.

## The Cities

### 🎸 Rock District
Home of the [[Crimson Amplified Order]]. Industrial architecture, raw energy, and guitar-shaped towers. Raw emotional combat style.

### 🎤 Hip-Hop Underground
Where the [[Sick 52]] built their rebel strongholds. The [[Verse Dominion]] thrives here. Layered streets, graffiti glyphs, and underground battle arenas.

### 🎼 Classical Heights
The [[Harmony Council]]'s seat of power. Home to the [[Sacred Conservatory]]. Pristine symmetrical architecture, cathedral-like concert halls, and strict order.

### 🎷 Jazz Quarter
Free-form, improvised architecture that shifts and changes. Home of the [[Covenant of Improvisation]]. Where [[Sync]] finds his path in Season 3.

### 🏠 Electronic Sector
Pulsing with synthesized beats. Home of the [[Pulse Order]] and the [[Synthetic Ascendancy]]. Minimalist geometric structures.

### 🥁 Afrobeat Grounds
Spiritual district with deep ancestral connection. Home of the [[Ancestral Rhythm Sect]]. Layered patterned architecture, drum towers.

### 🤘 Metal Metropolis
Heavy, industrial, armored. Forge-like structures and reinforced architecture. Extreme sound amplification zones.

## Political Significance

Each megacity functions as a semi-autonomous state with its own:
- **Genre Elder** — spiritual and political leader
- **Combat Academy** — training fighters in genre-specific techniques
- **Musical Courts** — legal system based on harmonic law
- **Export Economy** — each city produces its genre's music for the galaxy

The [[Single Beat System]] attempted to standardize all cities under one rhythm, creating massive political tension.
