![Intro The Dream Logo](/images/logos/logo_intro_the_dream.png)

## Overview
The birthplace of the universe. Water floats in midair. Cities exist as reflections in liquid mirrors. The sky is not a sky — it’s a cosmic ocean ceiling.

## Atmosphere and Aesthetic
* **Era:** Deep ocean blue
* **Visuals:** Deep ocean blue, silver light, soft cyan glow, ethereal, floating water
