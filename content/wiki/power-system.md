# Power System — Musical Abilities

On [[Planet Ongaku]], all power derives from **sound manipulation**. Every person possesses a base rhythmic frequency, and combat is an extension of musical expression.

## Core Abilities

Sound-based equivalents of traditional force powers:

| Ability | Effect | Equivalent |
|---------|--------|------------|
| **Sonic Shove** | Blast of compressed bass | Force Push |
| **Rhythm Remedy** | Healing through harmonic resonance | Force Heal |
| **Thunder Drop** | Lightning-frequency discharge | Force Lightning |
| **Frequency Shield** | Wall of stabilized sound | Force Barrier |
| **Bass Lock** | Immobilize targets through low-frequency paralysis | Force Grip |
| **Resonant Sense** | Detect all sound signatures in range | Force Sense |

## Elemental Spectrum

All musical powers align with one of seven elemental types:

- 🔥 **Fire** — Aggressive, chaotic frequency patterns (see [[Blaze Riff]])
- 🌍 **Earth** — Deep bass, seismic resonance (see [[Stone Resonance]], [[Tremor King]])
- 💧 **Water** — Flowing, adaptive sound waves (see [[Tidal Lament]])
- ⚡ **Electricity** — High-frequency discharge (see [[Thunder Dropper]])
- 🗡️ **Metal** — Rigid, structured frequencies (see [[Iron Tempo]])
- 🔊 **Sonic** — Pure sound manipulation (see [[Red Silence]], [[Bass Phantom]])
- ✨ **Arcane** — Reality-warping harmonic disruption (see [[Syncopate]])

## Power Classifications

### DJ24 Powers
Clean, controlled, stable. Cyan frequency rings expand symmetrically. The ground remains intact. [[DJ24]] members stabilize rather than destroy.

### Sick 52 Powers
Corrupted, chaotic, mutated. Powers leak and distort. The [[Founding Dissonants|Sick 52]] members cannot fully control their frequencies — their power damages them as much as their enemies.

### Genre Specialization
A fighter's religious [[Genre Megacities|genre affiliation]] affects their combat style. [[Crimson Amplified Order|Rock]] fighters favor aggressive strikes while [[Covenant of Improvisation|Jazz]] fighters use unpredictable improvisational patterns.
