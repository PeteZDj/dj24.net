# The Galaxy — Single-Craft Planets

> *"Every world masters one craft. Ongaku mastered sound — and sound rules them all."*

The galaxy of **DJ24: War of Sound** is built on a simple, powerful idea: **each planet
specializes in a single craft or industry**, and its place in the galaxy is defined by what it
exports. [[Planet Ongaku]] is the cultural superpower — the heartbeat the rest of the galaxy
depends on — but it is far from alone.

## Worlds That Shape the Story

### 🎶 Planet Ongaku — The Heartbeat
The music capital of the universe and the setting of the saga. Controlled by the
[[Harmony Council]], home of [[DJ24]], and torn between state-controlled sound and the
[[Sick 52]] underground. See [[Planet Ongaku]].

### 🎭 Planet Joke — Home of the Komedians
A world of ~150 million comedians governed like a never-ending improv show. It is the homeworld of
[[The Komedians]], who don't conquer worlds so much as **rewrite them into jokes**. In
[[Season 2|Season 2: The Komedian Invasion]], [[Sync]] is captured and forced to fight in its
"Joke Arena."

### 🔬 Planet Scientifica — First Contact
The galaxy's tech hub, split between cautious **Purists** and reckless **Evolvers**. A Scientifica
visitor first detected the frequency anomaly that opened the **HD Era**, and the scientist
**Dr. Aulon** later carries the warning that the Komedians are coming. Scientifica is Ongaku's
first real contact with the wider galaxy.

### 💰 Ecomerica · ⚙️ Fabriqua · ⏳ Chronos — The Journey Out
As the war winds down, **General 24** and **Billionaire X** travel to these worlds, opening the
saga toward the [[Eras of Ongaku|Galactic Era]] and the *Planet Ongaku* 4X strategy game.

## Three Scales of Place

The world is mapped at three nested scales — don't confuse them:

- **Districts / Cities** — neighborhoods of Ongaku (Classic City, Electric City, Rock City…). See [[Planet Ongaku]].
- **Planet** — Ongaku itself, plus the 13 other single-craft worlds above.
- **Galaxy** — the full set of worlds, connected (in the XD Era) by frequency-tunnel travel.
