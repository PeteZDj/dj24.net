# Master Arena List (57 Combat Stages)

This is the definitive guide to the combat arenas within the **War of Sound**. These stages are designed for high-intensity, cinematic encounters, blending the verticality of Shonen fighters with the environmental brutality of classic combat sims.

---

## 🌍 ONGAKU PRIME (CORE PLANET – CYBERPUNK / SHONEN)

### 1. Echo District Skyline
**Genre:** Cyberpunk metropolis  
**Description:** Skyscrapers with animated EQ bars running up the sides, neon rain, flying cars cutting through fog.  
**Hazards:** Bass shockwaves cause buildings to pulse.  
![Echo District Skyline](/images/cities/pop_city_1.png)

### 2. Frequency Grid Nexus
**Location:** Beneath Ongaku Prime  
**Genre:** Sci-fi core chamber  
**Layout:** Floating hex platforms over a glowing sound lattice.  
**Hazards:** Grid overload creates gravity flips.

### 3. Harmony Council Sky Chamber
**Location:** Floating fortress above clouds  
**Genre:** Authoritarian sci-fi  
**Layout:** Circular marble arena with holographic conductors.  
**Hazards:** Silence fields mute abilities.  
![Harmony Council Sky Chamber](/images/cities/cloud_city_1.png)

### 4. Echo Arena (Underground)
**Genre:** Illegal fight pit  
**Layout:** Circular pit surrounded by stacked speakers.  
**Hazards:** Crowd bass boosts fighters.

### 5. Bassline Borough Streets
**Genre:** Cyberpunk slum  
**Layout:** Narrow alleys, graffiti, exposed cables.  
**Hazards:** Sonic mines in pavement.  
![Bassline Borough Streets](/images/cities/urban_city_1.png)

### 6. Neon Market Rooftops
**Layout:** Multi-level rooftops with cloth banners.  
**Hazards:** Falling signage.  
![Neon Market Rooftops](/images/cities/rose_city_1.png)

### 7. Silent Archive Vault
**Genre:** Sacred / forbidden  
**Layout:** Endless black hall with floating vinyl records.  
**Hazards:** Total sound nullification.

### 8. DJ24 ONYX Command Deck
**Genre:** Military sci-fi  
**Layout:** Long command bridge, glass floor showing city below.  
**Hazards:** Alarm lockdowns.

### 9. Broken Sound Tower
**Genre:** Ruins  
**Layout:** Cracked spire leaning sideways.  
**Hazards:** Collapsing platforms.

### 10. Rain Cathedral
**Genre:** Cyber-religious  
**Layout:** Cathedral made of glass and sound waves.  
**Hazards:** Reverb storms.  
![Rain Cathedral](/images/cities/classic_city_1.png)

### 🔑 DESIGN ADVICE
*   **Verticality:** Storm-style games thrive on verticality & cinematic camera angles.
*   **Brutality:** MK-style games thrive on environmental brutality.
*   **Identity:** Every arena should have 1 visual identity + 1 gameplay gimmick.
*   **Storytelling:** Reuse lore: arenas should tell the story without dialogue.
