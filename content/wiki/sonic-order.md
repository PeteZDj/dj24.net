# The Sonic Order

The **Sonic Order** was the council of the 52 greatest musicians on [[Planet Ongaku]]. They were responsible for building and maintaining the [[Frequency Grid]] — the planetary system that stabilized reality and hid Ongaku from the galaxy.

## The 52 Masters

Each of the 52 masters maintained one node of the [[Frequency Grid]]. They were considered living legends — the pinnacle of musical achievement on Ongaku. Their combined power was enough to bend reality itself.

## The Betrayal

The [[Harmony Council]], fearing the Sonic Order's power, orchestrated their exile and disgrace. The 52 masters were:

1. Stripped of their official titles
2. Accused of "harmonic destabilization"
3. Exiled underground beneath [[Planet Ongaku]]
4. Left to rot in the silence beneath the surface

## The Mutation

In exile, the 52 masters didn't die. They **mutated**. Exposure to raw, unfiltered frequency energy transformed them — body and mind. They became the **[[Sick 52]]** — chaotic, powerful, and driven by vengeance.

## Legacy

The irony of the Sonic Order's fall is devastating:

- The masters who **built** the [[Frequency Grid]] are now the ones who could **destroy** it
- The [[Harmony Council]] created its own worst enemy
- [[NexaGen Harmonics]] later harvested the Sick 52's mutated DNA for corporate warfare
