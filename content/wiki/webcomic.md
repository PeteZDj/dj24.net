# DJ24: War of Sound — Webcomic

> *A weekly manga-style webcomic bringing the DJ battles to life.*

## The Concept

DJ24: War of Sound is being developed as an **ongoing webcomic** in the Webtoon/Manhwa vertical-scroll format, designed for mobile reading. The visual style blends **Cyberpunk aesthetics** with **Shonen anime action**.

---

## Preview Chapter — "The First Drop"

### Setting
📍 **Ongaku Prime — The Echo Arena**, an underground battleground where sound duels are fought.

### Characters
| Character | Role |
|---|---|
| **[[Sync]]** | DJ24 Rookie — new recruit, unsure of his true abilities |
| **[[Red Silence]]** | Sick 52 Leader — the most feared frequency manipulator |
| **[[General 24]]** | DJ24 Leader — watching from a command center, judging Sync's potential |

### The Plot
Sync must prove himself in his first real battle, but [[Red Silence]] is more powerful than expected. The chapter ends with a cryptic warning:

> *"You're not their savior, kid. You're their weapon."*

---

## Storyboard — Pages 1–10

### Page 1: Establishing the World
- Dark cityscape, bass drops echoing from distant buildings
- Cut to the massive **Echo Arena** underground
- Digital display: **"LIVE BATTLE: DJ24 vs. The Sick 52"**

### Pages 2–3: Fighters Enter
- **Sync** steps onto stage — DJ24 headset and battle gauntlets
- **Red Silence** walks in — calm, no visible speakers or gear
- Sync's inner monologue: *"No decks, no speakers? How does he fight?"*
- Red Silence raises his hand. The entire arena goes **silent**.

### Pages 4–6: Sound Erasure Attack
- Total silence — audience noise fades. The screen glitches. Words disappear.
- Sync panics — his speakers won't work.
- Red Silence smirks: *"You're already losing, rookie."*
- A pure frequency WAVE crashes into Sync, throwing him backward.

### Pages 7–9: Sync Fights Back
- Sync hits the ground. Vision flickering.
- Inner monologue: *"I can't hear. I can't fight. I have to… FEEL IT."*
- He touches the ground, syncing with vibrations. His heartbeat becomes the beat.
- **The ground beneath him THUMPS** — his rhythm resonates with the arena itself.
- Sync opens his eyes, body **pulsing with a low-frequency bassline**.
- *"You wanna erase sound? Then let's make some noise."*
- He SLAMS his foot — **BASS DROP** — a massive shockwave hits Red Silence.

### Page 10: Cliffhanger
- Red Silence takes the hit — flickers like an afterimage.
- For the first time, he looks **surprised**.
- He steps into the shadows: *"You might actually be interesting."*
- Final whisper: *"You're not their savior, kid. You're their weapon."*
- Sync breathes heavily. General 24 watches from the shadows.
- **TO BE CONTINUED…**

## Season 1 Arc Structure

| Arc | Chapters | Focus | Tone |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The First Drop** | 1–10 | Sync joins DJ24, first battle | High-energy intro |
| **The Echo Revolt** | 11–20 | Mavericks challenge General 24 | Internal conflict |
| **The Silence War** | 21–35 | Sick 52 full-scale attack | Dark, intense |
| **The Final Remix** | 36–50 | Sync discovers XDF, the truth. | Climax, betrayal |

Season 1 ends revealing the [[Harmony Council]] and [[NexaGen Harmonics]] have their own hidden agenda — setting up Season 2.
