# Races and Classes

The **DJ24 universe** contains three primary races and three combat classes. Hybrid subclasses create the full diversity of fighters across [[Planet Ongaku]] and beyond.

## Primary Races

### 1. Human
The dominant race on [[Planet Ongaku]]. All humans are trained in sound manipulation from birth. Their connection to music is biological — their very cells resonate with frequency.

### 2. Animal
Sentient animal species exist across the galaxy. On Ongaku, animal-human hybrids emerged through exposure to the [[Frequency Grid]]'s radiation. Many [[Sick 52]] members show animal hybrid traits — leopard markings ([[Harmony Eater]]), falcon features ([[Sync Destroyer]]), panther traits ([[Downbeat Reaper]]).

### 3. Sentient AI Machine
Artificial intelligences that achieved consciousness through musical frequency exposure. Sentient Machines range from android forms to massive mechanical war platforms.

## Hybrid Subclasses

| Hybrid | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| Human + Animal | Biological mutation, subtle bestial traits | [[Harmony Eater]] (leopard hybrid) |
| Animal + Machine | Mechanical beasts, AT Walker-type platforms | Resonance Striders |
| Machine + Animal | Predatory walkers, ambush platforms | Silence Widow spider mechs |
| Machine + Human | Piloted war platforms, Gundam-style | Cybernetic DJ24 members |
| Human + Machine | Surgical augmentation, droid hybrids | [[Sync Destroyer]], [[Chorus Tyrant]] |

## Combat Classes

### ⚔️ Warrior
Frontline fighters. Heavy weapons. Ground control. Physical frequency attacks — shockwaves, bass impacts, seismic stomps.

### 🔮 Mage
Frequency manipulators. Sound bending specialists. Area control through harmonic fields, illusion projection, and reality distortion. The [[Psychological Choir]] tier of the [[Sick 52]] exemplifies mage combat.

### 🗡️ Rogue Hybrid
Speed, assassination, and disruption. Rogue Hybrids blend biological and mechanical traits for unpredictable combat. [[Nocturne Prime]] with his dual scythes is a prime example.
