# Signature Moves

> *"On Ongaku, a move is a song you only get to play once. Miss the beat and you don't get a second verse."*

This page lists the **named signature moves** used throughout the [[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound webcomic]]. Each fighter on [[Planet Ongaku]] turns sound into a weapon in their own way. The moves below are referenced by name across the chapters, so you can look up exactly what a character did and how it works.

For the broader rules of how powers are classed, see [[Power System]] and [[Combat Styles|Combat Styles of Ongaku]].

---

## Sync (DJ24)

[[Sync]] is an anomaly. He never learned the standard catalogue of techniques, so he built his own around one idea: **if you can't hear the beat, feel it.**

### Resonance Sync
**Type:** Sensory / defensive.
Sync presses a hand or foot to a solid surface and reads the vibrations travelling through it. This lets him keep fighting even when an enemy has stolen all the sound from the air. It is the move that saved his life in his very first battle, when [[Red Silence]] silenced the whole arena.

### Bass Drop
**Type:** Offensive shockwave.
Sync stamps down and releases a low-frequency pulse — a **subwave** — that rolls outward along the ground and slams into everything in its path. It is his hardest-hitting attack and the first real technique he ever landed.

### On-Beat Transition
**Type:** Combo / stance-switch.
Most fighters pick one of the two basic stances — **Bass Stance** to hold ground and defend, or **Treble Stance** to strike fast and high. Sync instead switches between them at the exact moment of the downbeat, chaining a defensive move straight into a finisher. Timed right, the switch itself becomes the attack.

### Phantom Alignment
**Type:** Hidden ability (unrevealed).
The strange thing General 24 noticed about Sync: he doesn't play to a beat that already exists — he *aligns himself to one that isn't there yet.* No one, including Sync, understands this power yet. It is the seed of what the Sick 52 fearfully call the **Phantom**.

## Red Silence (Sick 52)

[[Red Silence]] is the founder of the [[Sick 52]] and the deadliest sound-warrior alive. He carries no gear at all. His entire style is built on taking sound *away* instead of making it.

### Sound Erasure
**Type:** Field control / signature.
With a flick of his fingers, Red Silence cancels every sound inside a wide zone. Music-based fighters suddenly can't use their powers, their gear goes dead, and the crowd falls silent. Inside this "mute field" he has every advantage. This is the move that opens his first fight with Sync.

### Dead Frequency
**Type:** Offensive shockwave (silent).
Red Silence stamps the ground and releases a wave of pure pressure. Because the wave makes no sound, there is no audible warning before it hits — the first sign you have that it's coming is when it throws you across the room.

### Null Step
**Type:** Movement / evasion.
He dissolves into static and reappears somewhere else, leaving only an afterimage behind. He uses it to slip out of a fight the instant it stops amusing him.

## Molly (DJ24)

[[Molly]], the **Shockwave Prima**, fights like a ballerina with blades. Her style turns elegant dance movements into brutal close-range strikes.

### Shockwave Pirouette
**Type:** Spinning melee attack.
Molly spins on point and lets her blades carry the momentum, throwing off rings of force with each rotation. The faster she spins, the wider the shockwaves.

### Prima Edge
**Type:** Weapon.
The twin retractable blades that extend from her forearms. They can ring off an incoming attack to parry it, or open up for a killing strike.

---

## Ninja Nagazaki (DJ24)

[[Ninja Nagazaki]] is DJ24's quiet executioner. Where Red Silence kills sound, Ninja kills the silence *between* sounds — striking in the gaps no one is listening to.

### Silent Frequency
**Type:** Bladed weapon.
A blade made of a razor-thin, near-invisible waveform. It cuts without a sound, which makes it almost impossible to block in time.

### Cutoff
**Type:** Assassination technique.
Ninja removes the sound from a small area around a single target, isolating them in a bubble of silence. Cut off from the city's noise, the target can't call for help and can't hear him coming.

## General 24 (DJ24)

[[General 24]] is the Supreme Commander of DJ24. He rarely fights with his hands at all.

### The 24-Hour Schedule
**Type:** Command / battlefield control.
General 24 treats a battle like a timetable. He positions his twenty-four guardians — one for every hour — so precisely that the enemy is always walking into the next "scheduled" event. As he puts it: *"I don't fight. I schedule reality."*

## The Sick 52

The mutated soldiers of the [[Deck of 52]] each carry one signature horror.

### Dead Air
**Type:** Field drain (Dead Air Revenant).
A low card of the [[Deck of 52|deck]], the **Dead Air Revenant**, spreads expanding zones of total silence. Left alone, the silence eats a whole district block by block — lights die, music stops, and the people inside slowly forget the sound was ever there.
