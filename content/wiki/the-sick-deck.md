# The Sick Deck — Deck of 52

> *"They erased our names. So we became cards — and we deal ourselves back into the world."*

Every member of the [[Sick 52]] is one card in a **52-card deck**. The system is inspired by the
legendary **"Deck of 52"** most-wanted mechanic from *Mercenaries: Playground of Destruction* —
re-tuned for a world where the targets are mutated sound-warlords. Browse it visually on the
**[Sick Deck](/sick-deck)** page.

---

## How the deck works

- **4 Houses (suits)** of **13 cards** each = 52.
- **Card-strength ladder** (weakest → strongest): `10 · 9 · 8 · 7 · 6 · 5 · 4 · 3 · 2` → **J · Q · K · A**.
  You hunt the **10 first** and climb to the **Ace**.
- **Aces** = apex bosses · **Kings / Queens / Jacks** = elite generals · **2–10** = field targets.
- **Every Queen is female.**
- **A♠ (Ace of Spades) = [Final Drop](/sick52/final-drop)** — the ultimate mutation, the strongest card in the deck.
- **The Joker** = the [[The Komedians|Komedian]] wildcard — an extra-dimensional threat outside the 52.

Each House is an internal **division** of the Sick 52 (their nature, not a military rank). The
player's own army, [[DJ24]], is organized differently — into four service branches (**Army, Navy,
Airforce, Space Force**); see [[DJ24: The Sick 52|the game page]].

## How it drives the story & the game

The deck isn't just a roster — it's the **campaign structure**. You pick a House to hunt, and work
the **most-wanted ladder** from the **10** (a first-contact mission in a new [[Planet Ongaku|city]])
up through the face cards to the **Ace**. Along the way you'll glimpse stronger cards early — meeting
a **2** (e.g. Bass Phantom) briefly before you're ready to face him.

See the playable design in **[[DJ24: The Sick 52]]** and the live targets on the
**[Sick Deck](/sick-deck)** board and the [[Sick 52]] roster.
