# The Sacred Conservatory — Classical

> *"Structure is divine. Order is enlightenment."*

## Philosophy
The Sacred Conservatory believes that the universe was created through **perfect composition** — not chaos. The [[Harmony Council]] draws heavily from Classical doctrine to justify the [[Single Beat System]].

## Clothing

### Everyday Followers
- High-collared **white and navy layered robes**
- Structured shoulder mantle
- Gold trim, clean lines, symmetrical patterns
- **Crest**: Treble clef encircled by laurel branches

### Elite (Maestro Cardinals)
- Long flowing robes, layered shoulder capes
- Baton-shaped energy rods
- Halo shaped like a treble clef

**Silhouette**: Tall, regal, angelic. Vatican meets battlefield conductor.

## Political Power
Classical Heights is the [[Harmony Council]]'s seat of power. The Sacred Conservatory is the most politically influential religion — their doctrine literally became law through the [[Single Beat System]]. This makes them simultaneously the most respected and most resented religion on [[Planet Ongaku]].
