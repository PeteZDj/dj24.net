# The Synthetic Ascendancy — Electronic

> *"Evolution through technology. The signal must advance."*

## Philosophy
The Synthetic Ascendancy believes sound must **evolve beyond organic limits**. They embrace augmentation, frequency technology, and the merger of flesh and machine. Many followers sympathize with the [[Sick 52]]'s ideology of sound evolution.

## Clothing

### Everyday Followers
- Augmented jackets with subtle tech details
- Modular utility belts, fingerless tech gloves
- Clean geometric patterns
- **Crest**: Sine wave with circuit nodes

### Elite (Signal Architects)
- Long digital coats with transparent panels
- Neural headgear
- Holographic interfaces

**Silhouette**: Thin, angular, machine-like. Techno prophets.

## Controversy
The Synthetic Ascendancy's beliefs align closely with [[NexaGen Harmonics]]' corporate agenda of weaponizing sound technology. This makes them politically suspect — are they a religion or a corporate front?
