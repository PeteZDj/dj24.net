# The Pulse Order — House

> *"Unity through rhythm. One pulse. One body. One floor."*

## Philosophy
The Pulse Order believes that all consciousness is connected through a **single universal beat**. Individual identity is secondary to collective rhythm. They seek transcendence through unity.

## Clothing

### Everyday Followers
- Dark **sleeveless long coats**, minimalist design
- Circular emblem at chest
- Simple black cloth pants, wrapped forearms
- **Crest**: Four symmetrical dots forming a square

### Elite
- Long clean cloak, subtle chest insignia
- No extra ornament — purity through simplicity

**Silhouette**: Calm, balanced, monk-like.
