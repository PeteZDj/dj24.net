# The Ancestral Rhythm Sect — Afrobeat

> *"Rhythm connects bloodlines. The drum is memory."*

## Philosophy
The Ancestral Rhythm Sect teaches that rhythm is **genetic memory**. Every beat pattern carries the knowledge of ancestors. Drumming is not performance — it is communion with the dead.

## Clothing

### Everyday Followers
- Layered **patterned fabrics** in earth tones
- Arm and neck beadwork
- Loose breathable tunics
- Symbolic drum-skin wraps
- **Crest**: Circular drum symbol

### Elite (Ancestral Pulsekeepers)
- Flowing ceremonial cloaks
- Mask-like face paint patterns
- Thick braided cords
- Energy drums strapped to backs

**Silhouette**: Grounded, circular, dynamic. Spiritual rhythm warriors.
