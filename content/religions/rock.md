# The Crimson Amplified Order — Rock

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "A group of warriors wearing long black cloaks with deep red inner lining, torn-edge hems, heavy boots, arm-wrapped bandages. Jagged lightning bolt crest splitting a circle. Naruto Akatsuki style but with ripped rock texture. Anime concept art." -->

> *"Emotion is sacred. Raw truth, unfiltered."*

## Philosophy
Followers of the Crimson Amplified Order believe that **raw emotional truth** is the highest form of divine expression. Restraint is sin. The [[Single Beat System]] is blasphemy. Every note should bleed.

## Clothing — Naruto/Bleach Aesthetic

### Everyday Followers
- Long **black cloaks** with deep **red inner lining**
- Torn-edge hems
- Heavy boots
- Arm-wrapped bandages
- **Crest**: A jagged lightning bolt splitting a circle

### Elite (High Voltage Saints)
- **Red shoulder mantles** over black cloaks
- Cloak stitched with cracked stone pattern
- Thick gauntlets
- Spiked shoulder details

**Silhouette**: Broad, aggressive, jagged. Warrior priests of thunder.

## Influence
Strong in **Rock District** of [[Planet Ongaku]]. Rock followers favor aggressive strikes, raw power, and overwhelming force in combat. Several [[DJ24]] members follow this doctrine, creating internal ideological tension with [[Sacred Conservatory|Classical]] members.
