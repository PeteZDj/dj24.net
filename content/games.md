# Video Game Universe

Five games spanning the full history of [[Planet Ongaku]], each covering a different era and genre.

---

## DJ Battle
**Low-Res Era · Tactical Rhythm Combat**

Dojo building, tournament progression, and 1v1 DJ battles in the Golden Age of Sound. Experience the era when music was pure and the 52 Masters created the [[Frequency Grid]].

---

## DJ VIP
**Early-HD Era · Narrative Social Sim**

Navigate fame, corruption, and the music industry's fall from grace. Watch as the seeds of the [[Harmony Council]]'s rise are planted and the industry becomes political.

---

## DJ24
**XD Era · Open-World Action RPG**

Cyberpunk city-building, real-time sound combat, and interdimensional warfare. Play as [[Sync]] and fight across [[Planet Ongaku]] during the most explosive era.

---

## DJ24: The Sick 52
**Mid-XD Era · Deck-of-52 Bounty War**

A most-wanted **bounty war** built on the [[The Sick Deck|Deck of 52]]. Hunt all 52 enemies — dealt
as a four-house deck — across the cities of [[Planet Ongaku]], fielding the four [[DJ24]] branches
(Army, Navy, Airforce, Space Force). Work each house from the **10 → Ace**.

→ Read the full design on **[[DJ24: The Sick 52]]** · browse the **[Missions board](/missions)** and
the **[Sick Deck](/sick-deck)**.

---

## Planet Ongaku
**Galactic Era · Space 4X Strategy**

Explore the cosmos. Diplomacy, conquest, and music-based civilization management. Take Ongaku's sound to the stars.
