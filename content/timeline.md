# The Ages of Ongaku

<!-- IMAGE_PROMPT: "An epic timeline mural showing the evolution of a musical planet, from primitive sound duels to neon cyberpunk cities to galactic warfare, panoramic horizontal composition, anime concept art style" -->

The history of [[Planet Ongaku]] spans five major eras, from the dawn of pure sound to intergalactic war.

---

## Pre-Rhythm (PR) / Low-Res Era
**The Golden Age of Music Battles**

Before control, before structure. Music was wild and pure, shaping reality itself across the cosmos. Pure artistic mastery, dojos, and rhythm duels without tech.

The **52 Masters** (later the [[Sick 52]]) built the [[Frequency Grid]] and the Sound Towers during this era. No guns, no tech — just sound.

---

## Early-HD Era
**The Era of Fame and Corruption**

The industry falls under corporate control. High-tech advancements begin. This is the era where music starts becoming political, and the seeds of the [[Harmony Council]]'s rise are planted.

---

## HD Era
**The Great Betrayal**

The [[Harmony Council]] rises, strips the 52 Masters of their power, and enforces the **Single Beat System (SBS)**. The [[Sick 52]] are born from this betrayal.

[[NexaGen Harmonics]] also rises during this era, beginning to weaponize music commercially.

---

## XD (Extra-Dimensions) Era
**The Interdimensional Wars**

The [[Frequency Grid]] falls during the war between [[DJ24]] and the [[Sick 52]]. This breach allows [[The Komedians]] to invade from Planet Joke.

[[DJ24]] evolves from a planetary force into a prominent cosmic one. [[Sync]]'s powers as the [[Syncopate]] fully awaken during this era.

---

## Post-XD / Galactic Era
**The Universe Expands**

Sound warfare expands to the stars. [[Planet Ongaku]] becomes a universal power. New planets, new alliances, and cosmic-scale conflict over the future of music.
