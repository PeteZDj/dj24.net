## Overview

Gifted but conflicted. Hears patterns others can't. Questions everything.

[[Sync]] is the protagonist of **DJ24: War of Sound** — a rare [[Syncopate]] born with the power to "break the beat" in a society governed by the [[Harmony Council]]'s rigid **Single Beat System (SBS)**.

He possesses **Harmonic Disruption**: the ability to break, remix, and reshape sound waves, altering music and reality itself. His natural XDF (Extra-Dimensional Frequency) abilities make him one of the most powerful beings on [[Planet Ongaku]].

---

## Abilities

- **Harmonic Disruption** — Break, remix, and reshape sound waves
- **XDF Sensitivity** — Natural ability to perceive and manipulate Extra-Dimensional Frequencies
- **Pattern Recognition** — Hears rhythmic patterns invisible to others
- **Reality Manipulation** — At full power, can alter the fabric of reality through sound

## Story Arc

- **[[Season 1|Season 1: The Internal War]]** — Joins [[DJ24]]. Discovers his Syncopate nature during the war against the [[Sick 52]].
- **[[Season 2|Season 2: The Komedian Invasion]]** — Captured by [[The Komedians]] and taken to Planet Joke. Survives the interdimensional nightmare.
- **[[Season 3|Season 3: The AI War]]** — Feeling lost, wanders and explores freeform Jazz and rebel Rockers, finding his own musical identity.
- **[[Season 4|Season 4: The Forgotten Soldiers]]** — Acts as an anti-hero, hunting the worst [[Sick 52]] remnants.
