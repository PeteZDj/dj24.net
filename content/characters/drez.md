## Overview

Chaotic energy. Speaks in freestyle rap. Doesn't follow orders.

[[Drez]] is the leader of **The Mavericks**, one of [[DJ24]]'s three internal factions. Where [[General 24]] values discipline, Drez embodies pure chaos. He doesn't just want to protect [[Planet Ongaku]] — he wants to **rule** it.
