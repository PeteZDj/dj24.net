## Overview

Tactical genius. Feared and respected. Believes in absolute efficiency and structure. [[General 24]] is the strict, calculated commander of [[DJ24]], and the son of the mysterious **Billionaire X** who founded the organization.

He values precise order above all else, and his leadership style clashes with the rebellious nature of [[Sync]] and the chaos of [[Drez]].

## Story Arc

- **[[Season 1|Season 1: The Internal War]]** — Commands DJ24 against the [[Sick 52]], struggling to maintain unity as internal factions emerge.
- **[[Season 3|Season 3: The AI War]]** — Disillusioned by endless conflict, allies with [[NexaGen Harmonics]]'s AI musicians to create an unstoppable synthetic army.
- **[[Season 4|Season 4: The Forgotten Soldiers]]** — Explores intergalactic politics as DJ24 fractures completely.
