**"The First Drop"** is the first chapter of *[[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound]]* and the opening chapter of **Arc 1 — The First Drop**.

## Summary

On [[Planet Ongaku]], sound is not just something you hear — it is the power that runs the entire world. Every wall, every light, and every heartbeat in the city is carried by a hidden lattice called the [[Frequency Grid]]. The people who protect that power are the guardians of [[DJ24]], and the people trying to switch it off forever are the [[Sick 52]]. For years the two sides have fought a quiet war over a single question: should the world keep making noise, or finally fall silent?

The chapter opens at night in the neon capital of Ongaku Prime. Rain falls across the streets while a deep bass hum rolls out from a distant tower. Far beneath the city, in an illegal underground fight pit called the [[Echo Arena|Echo Arena]], a live battle is about to begin between DJ24 and the Sick 52.

A new DJ24 recruit named [[Sync]] steps onto the stage. His headset and battle gauntlets hum to life, but the announcer warns the crowd that the rookie has been matched against the deadliest sound manipulator alive. Across from him walks [[Red Silence]], the founder of the Sick 52. Unlike every other fighter, Red Silence carries no speakers, no decks, and no gear at all. Sync has no idea how a man with no equipment is supposed to fight.

He finds out quickly. Red Silence raises one hand and uses [[Sound Erasure]], cancelling every sound in the arena at once. The roar of the crowd vanishes, the screens glitch out, and Sync's gauntlets go completely dead — without sound, his music-based powers are useless. Before he can recover, Red Silence stamps the ground and releases [[Dead Frequency]], a silent shockwave of pure pressure that gives no warning before it slams into Sync and throws him across the floor.

Lying on the ground, unable to hear and unable to fight the normal way, Sync realises he has to stop listening and start *feeling*. He presses his palm flat to the floor and uses [[Resonance Sync]], reading the vibrations travelling through the ground. He tunes himself to his own heartbeat until his rhythm begins to resonate with the arena itself. His eyes snap open, and for the first time his whole body pulses with a low bassline. Telling Red Silence that if he wants to erase sound then they should make some noise, Sync drives his foot down and unleashes [[Bass Drop]] — a subwave that erupts straight at his opponent.

Red Silence takes the hit head-on. His body flickers like an afterimage, and for the first time ever, he looks genuinely surprised. As sound slowly returns to the arena, Sync is still standing. Impressed, Red Silence admits the rookie might actually be interesting, then uses [[Null Step]] to dissolve into static and slip away. Before he disappears, he leaves Sync with a warning he doesn't understand: he is not the city's savior — he is its weapon. High above the pit, hidden in the dark, [[General 24]] watches the whole fight in silence.
