**"First Blood, First Drop"** is the tenth chapter of *[[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound]]* and the **finale of Arc 1 — The First Drop**.

## Summary

Beyond the great door lies a cavern of black speaker-cones, and from it rises [[Bass Phantom]], the Sub-Bass Titan — a giant whose chest is an open, resonating pit. He sneers that [[DJ24]] sent a mere child to face the Jack of Spades, and promises to fold [[Sync]] into the floor. Sync stands his ground, telling the Titan that he is the one who once buried [[MasterBass]]'s entire order in silence, and that Sync has been training for exactly this fight.

Bass Phantom slams both fists down and unleashes [[Bass Quake]], a shockwave that flattens the whole vault. The rest of the squad is hurled back, but Sync drops low into the [[Resonance Anchor]] and rides it out, remembering the monk's words: don't push back, be heavier than the wave.

Then Sync does something no one has managed before — he begins to walk straight into the sub-bass that flattens everyone else. The Titan is stunned, insisting that no one can stand in his pressure. Sync corrects him: he isn't standing *in* it, he's standing *on the beat underneath it*. For a brief moment, his outline splits into faint, misaligned afterimages as his hidden power, [[Phantom Alignment]], begins to stir. He finds the half-beat the Titan can't strike on and slips inside his guard, leaving Bass Phantom genuinely afraid and demanding to know what he is.

With the giant's open chest-resonator now in reach, Sync jams both gauntlets into the cavity and unloads a [[Counter-Drop]] — turning the Titan's own bass all the way up and firing it straight back into him. The resonator overloads, and the Sub-Bass Titan topples. On the deck board, the Jack of Spades flips face-down: the squad's first face card has fallen.

But the victory comes with a warning. The broken Titan laughs softly, telling Sync that he is only one Jack out of fifty-two, and that this was never the war — it was merely the tuning note. High on a ledge above, [[Red Silence]] watches, unsurprised and almost proud. He congratulates Sync on his "first blood, first drop," and tells him to keep climbing, because the Aces are awake now. With that, the opening arc of the [[Webcomic|War of Sound]] comes to a close.
