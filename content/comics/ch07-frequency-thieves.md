**"Frequency Thieves"** is the seventh chapter of *[[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound]]*, continuing **Arc 1 — The First Drop**.

## Summary

[[DJ24]] runs a routine supply mission across the cargo skybridges of [[Ongaku Prime]]. The cargo is a "drop" — a bright capsule of stored sound-energy — gliding along a guarded sky-rail. [[Molly]] gives [[Sync]] simple instructions: get the drop to the spire before sunrise, and no heroics.

The mission falls apart immediately. A curved blade of compressed sound slices the rail, and a one-eyed pirate snatches the falling capsule out of the air. This is [[SubZ]], a battle-scarred frequency pirate who, as he cheerfully explains, doesn't erase your sound like the [[Sick 52]] do — he simply steals it. Molly recognises him at once and shouts at Sync not to lose the drop.

Sync sprints down the rain-soaked skybridge after him, leaping the gaps between cargo containers. But every time he attacks, it backfires: SubZ's [[Waveform Cutlass]] uses a technique called [[Drop Theft]], swallowing Sync's [[Bass Drop]] whole and hurling it right back at him. Sync quickly realises that the pirate doesn't block attacks — he feeds on them, and every blast of bass only makes him stronger.

So Sync changes tactics. He stops throwing power entirely and plants his feet, using the [[Resonance Anchor]] he learned from [[MasterBass]] to go still and heavy. With nothing to steal, SubZ overcommits to a wild swing at empty air, and Sync simply steps inside his guard and grabs hold of the stolen capsule. As the two of them strain over the drop at the very edge of the bridge, Sync notices the pirate's crew behind him — thin, hungry, and clearly poor.

SubZ explains himself. The Frequency Grid feeds the wealthy spire while letting the dockside districts go dark, so his crew steals just to keep their own neighbourhood's lights and music alive. Sync offers him a better target: the Sick 52 are about to make *every* district go dark, permanently. After a long look, SubZ lets go of the drop, grins, and agrees that his crew will fight — for pay. Molly notes that [[General 24]] is going to hate this, and that she loves it. With that, the pirate becomes the squad's newest, most unlikely guardian.
