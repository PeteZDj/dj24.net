# Webcomic — AI Image Prompts

This is the master reference of every AI image prompt used for the
*[[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound]]* webcomic. Each chapter's cover (and any
interior storyboard art) is generated from these prompts. The same prompts are
shown as captions beneath each image in the reader.

> **Note:** Chapters 5–10 list the exact prompts used. Chapters 1–4 covers and
> interior panels were generated earlier; their prompts here are faithful
> reconstructions of the original art direction.

---

## Shared style anchor

Every prompt below is generated on top of this shared style anchor (it keeps all
chapters visually consistent):

> Cyberpunk shonen manga / Korean manhwa webcomic art for 'DJ24: The War of Sound'. Neon-soaked future music city. Visible SOUND made physical — concentric bass shockwave rings, glowing waveform ribbons, equalizer light, frequency distortion. Bold confident anime linework, dramatic high-contrast cel shading, deep blacks, vivid neon rim lighting (electric cyan, magenta, gold, deep blue). Cinematic dynamic angle, strong silhouettes, motion lines, energy and impact. Polished premium digital color, high detail, action-poster composition. No text, no speech bubbles, no watermark, no logo.

## Chapter 1 — The First Drop

**Cover (`ch01-cover.png`):** The hero Sync (young white male, messy short hair, white split-tail coat with red inner lining, glowing electric-blue gauntlets) faces Red Silence (calm hooded Sick 52 founder in dark robes, no gear of any kind) across a packed underground Echo Arena ringed with towering stacked speaker walls and roaring crowd. Between them, a wall of glowing bass shockwave rings on Sync's side meets a void of dead grey silence on Red Silence's side.

**Page 1 (`ch01-establishing.png`):** Wide establishing shot of a dark cyberpunk music metropolis at night, rain-slick streets bleeding neon, towering holographic billboards and equalizer-bar skyscrapers, a deep bass hum rolling out from a distant megastructure, then the camera plunging beneath the city toward a vast underground fight arena ringed with stacked speakers.

**Pages 4–6 (`ch01-erasure.png`):** Inside the Echo Arena, Red Silence (hooded Sick 52 founder, dark robes) raises one hand and erases all sound — the giant arena screens glitch and go blank, the crowd falls silent, and the rookie Sync's electric-blue gauntlets flicker and die mid-motion. A cold grey nullified silence spreads outward in a flat dead ring while Sync staggers, powerless.

**Pages 7–9 (`ch01-bassdrop.png`):** Low dramatic angle: Sync (white split-tail coat with red lining, glowing electric-blue gauntlets) drives his foot down and unleashes a massive bass drop — an enormous electric-blue subwave erupting along the arena floor straight toward Red Silence, debris and dust blasting upward, his eyes blazing with rediscovered rhythm. Pure impact and momentum.

Sync (white split-tail coat with red lining, electric-blue gauntlets) and Molly (silver forearm blades) stand below it, lit by neon and the card-board glow.

Gritty, kinetic, neon-lit.
