**"The Sub-Bass Monk"** is the sixth chapter of *[[Webcomic|DJ24: The War of Sound]]*, continuing **Arc 1 — The First Drop**.

## Summary

Back at the Command Spire in [[Ongaku Prime]], [[General 24]] examines the playing card recovered from the silent district in Blue City. It is a spade, and it confirms his fear: the [[Dead Air Revenant]] was sent as a test by a much higher-ranked enemy — a sub-bass fighter. He warns [[Sync]] that against an enemy who controls crushing low frequencies, his [[Bass Drop]] will be worthless, because the enemy will simply absorb it. When Sync asks to be taught how to beat sub-bass, the General admits he cannot teach it himself. Only one person can, and he warns Sync he won't enjoy the lessons.

That person is [[MasterBass]], an ancient, frail monk who lives at a quiet temple above the city. When Sync arrives, he finds a tiny old man sweeping the steps and can hardly believe this is the legendary Sub-Frequency Enforcer. MasterBass cuts him off before he can be rude, telling him that he is too loud, and that loud people are always the first to break.

To prove the point, the monk taps his staff once and unleashes [[Sub-Bass Pressure]] — a wave of twenty-hertz sound too low to hear but heavy enough to stop a heart. Sync is instantly driven to his knees, unable to move. MasterBass explains his mistake: Sync is trying to fight the wave, which is as foolish as trying to out-shout the ocean. Instead, he must become heavier than it. He must put his rhythm down into the ground rather than out into the air — he must learn to anchor himself.

Sync stops straining and presses both palms flat against the stone, reaching for the [[Frequency Grid]] beneath it with [[Resonance Sync]]. Instead of resisting the deep hum, he matches his own pulse to it, and the pressure begins to flow around him rather than through him. Slowly, for the first time, he rises and stands tall inside the crushing field. This new defensive technique is the [[Resonance Anchor]]. MasterBass nods, satisfied: now that Sync can stand in the storm, the next lesson will be learning to walk through it.

As the sun sets over the temple, the old monk finally explains why this matters. The enemy Sync will soon face is the **Sub-Bass Titan**, a Jack of the spade suit, who once buried MasterBass's entire order in silence. Hearing that their next target is a face card, Sync realises how high the squad is now climbing up the [[Deck of 52|deck]].
